# Veterinary Medicines Regulations Annex 2 Requirements - Section I General principles and requirements

## One-Line Summary

UK VMD guidance says a marketing authorisation dossier must meet the requirements of the annex and reflect up-to-date veterinary medicinal knowledge and relevant quality, safety, and efficacy guidelines.

## Why It Matters For CKD

This is useful for translating the CKD internal evidence base into dossier expectations.

## Key Findings

### quoted_fact

- Documentation accompanying a marketing authorisation application must be presented in accordance with Annex requirements.
- Applicants must take into account up-to-date veterinary medicinal knowledge and relevant quality, safety, and efficacy guidelines.

### source_supported_conclusion

- UK pathway planning cannot stop at route selection; dossier structure and current scientific guidance alignment are explicit expectations.

### llm_inference

- The internal CKD system could later become a dossier-preparation support layer if evidence objects are kept clean enough.

## Limits / Caveats

- This source states principles, not CKD-specific evidentiary thresholds.
