# DailyMed Current Label: Senvelgo (velagliflozin oral solution)

## One-Line Summary

Current DailyMed animal-drug label for Senvelgo, used to control indication, warning, dosing, storage, and monitoring language for the feline diabetes SGLT2 branch.

## Key Findings

- DailyMed lists Senvelgo as a prescription animal drug label with NADA 141-568.
- The label is for velagliflozin oral solution 15 mg/mL for oral use in cats only.
- The label was updated April 1, 2026, with version 5 and effective time 20260401.
- The indication is to improve glycemic control in otherwise healthy cats with diabetes mellitus not previously treated with insulin.
- The label contains a diabetic ketoacidosis/euglycemic ketoacidosis warning.
- The label says not to use Senvelgo in cats previously treated with insulin, receiving insulin, or with insulin-dependent diabetes mellitus.
- Senvelgo is supplied in 12 mL and 30 mL fill volumes with a dosing syringe.
- The label states storage at or below 77°F / 25°C, with excursions permitted up to 104°F / 40°C, and six-month use after opening.
