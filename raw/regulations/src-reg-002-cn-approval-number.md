# 兽药产品批准文号管理办法

## One-Line Summary

China requires a veterinary product approval number from the ministry before a manufacturer can produce a specific veterinary product for market.

## Why It Matters For CKD

This matters because registration is not the end of the path. Product-specific market manufacture still ties back to approval-number management.

## Key Findings

### quoted_fact

- Veterinary manufacturers must obtain a veterinary product approval number to produce a veterinary product.
- The approval number is issued by the Ministry based on national standards, production process, and production conditions.
- Applicants for a self-developed product with a new veterinary drug registration certificate can apply using the required supporting materials listed in the measure.

### source_supported_conclusion

- The China path should be modeled as at least a two-step regulatory logic: registration plus product approval-number execution.

### llm_inference

- Later pathway notes should explicitly separate “regulatory approval logic” from “commercial manufacture readiness”.

## Limits / Caveats

- This source governs approval-number management, not efficacy requirements by indication.
