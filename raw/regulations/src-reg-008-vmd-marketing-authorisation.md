# Marketing authorisations for veterinary medicines

## One-Line Summary

The UK VMD states that veterinary products requiring a marketing authorisation must use one of several authorisation routes, including a national GB route and routes connected to European procedures for Northern Ireland.

## Why It Matters For CKD

This is the first official UK pathway anchor for a feline CKD product.

## Key Findings

### quoted_fact

- If a product requires a marketing authorisation, applicants must apply to the VMD.
- The guidance describes five routes that can be used to obtain a marketing authorisation in the UK or part of it.

### source_supported_conclusion

- The UK branch should be modeled as a route-selection problem early, not as a single generic pathway.

### llm_inference

- For a feline CKD product, GB-focused planning may be simpler as an initial UK frame than trying to solve all jurisdictional variants at once.

## Limits / Caveats

- This is route-overview guidance, not efficacy-package detail.
