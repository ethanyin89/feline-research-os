# 兽药注册办法

## One-Line Summary

China's core registration rule states that new veterinary drug registration and imported veterinary drug registration are regulated at the ministry level and require completion of clinical testing before formal submission.

## Why It Matters For CKD

This is one of the primary legal anchors for any China pathway discussion for a feline CKD product.

## Key Findings

### quoted_fact

- New veterinary drug registration and imported veterinary drug registration are governed by this measure.
- New veterinary drug applicants should submit after completion of clinical trials.
- The Ministry level authority is responsible for national veterinary drug registration.

### source_supported_conclusion

- For a CKD product targeting cats in China, the working assumption should be a formal veterinary registration path rather than an informal companion-animal shortcut.

### llm_inference

- There may be pathway differences depending on whether the product is a domestic new veterinary drug or an imported veterinary medicine, so later regulatory mapping should branch early on this question.
