# 兽药进口管理办法

## One-Line Summary

Imported veterinary drugs in China are subject to dedicated import management, including import customs documentation and product documentation requirements.

## Why It Matters For CKD

Any attempt to bring an overseas feline CKD product into China will need an import branch, not just a domestic registration mindset.

## Key Findings

### quoted_fact

- Imported veterinary drugs require an import customs clearance form.
- Applications require contracts, business documentation, product inspection report, shipping documents, and Chinese labeling and instructions.

### source_supported_conclusion

- Imported feline CKD products should be treated as a separate regulatory workflow in the internal map.

### llm_inference

- “Human drug to pet” discussion in China should not be assumed to be a simple relabeling problem, because import and veterinary administration layers still apply.

## Limits / Caveats

- This is import-administration focused, not an efficacy review guide.
