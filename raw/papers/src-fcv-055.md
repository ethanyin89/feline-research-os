# FCV Prevalence in FORL and Chronic Gingivostomatitis

## Evidence-Depth Caveat

Abstract-weighted extraction. Full text needed for diagnostic methods and case definitions.

## One-Line Summary

FCV detected in 60% of FCGS cats vs 24% controls (p=0.003); no association with FORL.

## Why It Matters For Feline FCV

Quantifies FCV-gingivostomatitis association; clarifies FORL is not FCV-associated.

## Key Findings

### quoted_fact

- Control cats: 24.0% FCV positive
- FORL cats: 22.5% FCV positive (no difference from control)
- FCGS cats: 60.0% FCV positive
- p = 0.003 for overall comparison

### source_supported_conclusion

- FCGS has strong FCV association
- FORL is not associated with FCV
