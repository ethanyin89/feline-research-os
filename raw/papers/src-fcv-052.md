# Optimal Sampling Sites for FURTD Pathogen Detection

## Evidence-Depth Caveat

Abstract-weighted extraction. Full text needed for sensitivity/specificity data.

## One-Line Summary

Oropharynx is best single sampling site for FCV (92.3%), FHV-1, and Chlamydia detection in FURTD cats.

## Why It Matters For Feline FCV

Practical diagnostic guidance; optimizes sample collection for respiratory panel testing.

## Key Findings

### quoted_fact

- 104 cats with FURTD sampled at 4 sites
- FCV prevalence: 50.0% (real-time PCR)
- FCV detection by site: oropharynx 92.3%, tongue 90.4%, conjunctiva 38.5%
- Multiple sites significantly increase detection rate

### source_supported_conclusion

- Oropharynx is optimal if single sample only
- Multi-site sampling improves sensitivity for all pathogens
