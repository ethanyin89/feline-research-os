# FCV Distribution and Diversity in Moscow

## Evidence-Depth Caveat

Abstract-weighted extraction. Full text needed for complete phylogenetic analysis.

## One-Line Summary

FCV prevalence 25.7% in Moscow (2018-2021); field strains show 77-83% similarity to vaccines.

## Why It Matters For Feline FCV

Documents vaccine-field strain mismatch; rising prevalence trend concerning.

## Key Findings

### quoted_fact

- 6,213 samples tested, 25.7% FCV positive
- Prevalence trend: 18.9% → 32.6% over 2018-2021
- Most isolates cluster with Chinese strains
- 77-83% nucleotide similarity to vaccine strains

### source_supported_conclusion

- Substantial vaccine-field strain divergence
- FCV prevalence may be increasing in Moscow
