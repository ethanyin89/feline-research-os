## One-Line Summary

2016 JVIM study validating cystatin C as biomarker for detecting chronic kidney disease in cats.

## Why It Matters For Feline CKD

Cystatin C as alternative biomarker:
1. Not affected by muscle mass (unlike creatinine)
2. May detect CKD in cats where creatinine is misleading
3. Complements SDMA (src-ckd-052) for multi-marker approach
