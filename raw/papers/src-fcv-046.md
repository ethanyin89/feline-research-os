# Feline IRF1 as antiviral factor against FCV

## Evidence-Depth Caveat

Abstract-weighted extraction. Full text needed for ISG screening methodology.

## One-Line Summary

Feline IRF1 inhibits FCV replication; FCV evades immunity by reducing IRF1 expression.

## Why It Matters For Feline FCV

Identifies host antiviral mechanism and viral immune evasion strategy; potential therapeutic target.

## Key Findings

### quoted_fact

- IRF1 significantly inhibits FCV replication (other ISGs tested did not)
- IRF1 localizes to nucleus, activates IFN-β and ISRE promoter
- FCV infection reduces IRF1 mRNA and protein levels
- IRF1 also active against FPV, FHV, FIPV

### source_supported_conclusion

- IRF1 is cross-protective against multiple feline viruses
- FCV has evolved IRF1 suppression as immune evasion strategy
