2010 JFMS review covering diagnosis and management of systemic hypertension in cats.

## Why It Matters For Feline CKD

Blood pressure control in CKD:
1. Hypertension accelerates CKD progression
2. Untreated hypertension causes target organ damage (eyes, heart, brain)
3. Treatment decisions must balance renal and cardiovascular considerations
