# The Cat as a Model for Human Obesity and Diabetes

## Evidence-Depth Caveat

**Deep-extracted from PMC full text (PMC3440058).** 2012 J Diabetes Sci Technol review by Hoenig M: establishes cat as translational model for human T2DM and obesity. Key parallels: peripheral insulin resistance, amyloid pathology, adipokine changes. Key limitation: no atherosclerosis/hypertension. Evidence level: narrative review with extensive mechanistic detail.

## Clinical Testing Limitations

**Quoted:** "Measurement of insulin resistance alone will not allow us to predict progression to diabetes, neither will glucose tolerance testing with pharmacological amounts of glucose."

**Stress hyperglycemia:** "Many client-owned cats have a high incidence of stress hyperglycemia regardless of body condition."
