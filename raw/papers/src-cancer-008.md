## Key Findings

### quoted_fact

- The intake sheet lists this title: The Histologic Classification of 602 Cases of Feline Lymphoproliferative Disease using the National Cancer Institute Working Formulation.
- The intake sheet locator is: 10.1177/104063870001200401.
- The study reviewed 688 admissions from 12 veterinary institutions.
- The final analysis used 602 cases.
- The cases were categorized as 69 low-grade, 210 intermediate-grade, and 323 high-grade lymphomas.
- FeLV antigen status was unknown or untested in most admissions.
