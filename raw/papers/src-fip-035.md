# Original GS-441524 Preclinical Efficacy Study

## Evidence-Depth Caveat

Title-only intake. Full text needed for detailed efficacy data.

## One-Line Summary

2018 LANDMARK preclinical study by Murphy and Pedersen demonstrating GS-441524 strongly inhibits FIP virus in tissue culture and experimental cat infection, establishing foundation for clinical treatment.

## Why It Matters For Feline FIP

Foundation of FIP treatment revolution:
1. FIRST demonstration that GS-441524 inhibits FIP virus
2. Both in vitro (tissue culture) and in vivo (cat infection) evidence
3. Direct precursor to clinical trials that changed FIP from fatal to treatable
