# FCV and FHV-1 Isolation in Southern Brazil

## Evidence-Depth Caveat

Abstract-weighted extraction. Full text needed for strain characterization.

## One-Line Summary

In Southern Brazil, FCV isolated from 52.7% of positive cats; FHV-1 from 38.2%; co-infections 9.1%.

## Why It Matters For Feline FCV

Regional epidemiology for South America; FCV more common than FHV-1.

## Key Findings

### quoted_fact

- 302 cats sampled (2006-2009)
- 55 cats positive for FCV and/or FHV-1
- FCV alone: 52.7%
- FHV-1 alone: 38.2%
- Co-infection: 9.1%

### source_supported_conclusion

- FCV dominant respiratory pathogen in region
- Co-infection rate ~10%
