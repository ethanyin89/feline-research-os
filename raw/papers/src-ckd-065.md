# Feline Hyperparathyroidism Review

## Evidence-Depth Caveat

Title-only intake. Full text needed for diagnostic criteria and treatment protocols.

## One-Line Summary

2015 JFMS review covering primary and secondary hyperparathyroidism in cats, including CKD-associated secondary disease.

## Why It Matters For Feline CKD

Secondary hyperparathyroidism is a key CKD complication:
1. Contributes to mineral bone disorder (CKD-MBD)
2. Affects prognosis and quality of life
3. Connects to phosphorus management and FGF-23 studies (src-ckd-026, 056, 058)
