# ERA-LFD Rapid FCV Detection Method

## Evidence-Depth Caveat

Abstract-weighted extraction. Full text needed for validation data.

## One-Line Summary

ERA-LFD achieves 3.2 TCID50 sensitivity for FCV detection via isothermal amplification with lateral flow readout.

## Why It Matters For Feline FCV

Rapid point-of-care diagnostic option; no thermocycler required.

## Key Findings

### quoted_fact

- Targets FCV-ORF1 conserved region
- Sensitivity: 3.2 TCID50/reaction
- Isothermal amplification (no PCR equipment)
- Visual lateral flow dipstick readout

- Highly sensitive rapid test
- Field-deployable diagnostic
