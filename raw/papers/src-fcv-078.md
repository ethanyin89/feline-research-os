# France Nosocomial VS-FCV Outbreak 2005

## Evidence-Depth Caveat

Abstract-weighted extraction. Full text needed for infection control details and genetic analysis.

## One-Line Summary

Nosocomial VS-FCV outbreak at Toulouse veterinary hospital; 8 cats infected, 5 died/euthanized, strain distinct from US/UK.

## Why It Matters For Feline FCV

Documents hospital-acquired VS-FCV transmission; highlights infection control challenges and distinct strain emergence.

## Key Findings

### quoted_fact

- 8 cats infected in hospital outbreak
- 62.5% mortality (5/8)
- Virus spread outside hospital to students' cats
- Genetically distinct from US/UK strains

### source_supported_conclusion

- VS-FCV can spread nosocomially
- Infection control critical in veterinary facilities
