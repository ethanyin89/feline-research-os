# VS-FCV China Strain Produces Broad Neutralizing Antibodies

## Evidence-Depth Caveat

Abstract-weighted extraction. Full text needed for neutralization titer data.

## One-Line Summary

Chinese VS-FCV strain HBDL2 elicits broad-spectrum neutralizing antibodies covering GI, GII, and VS-FCV strains.

## Why It Matters For Feline FCV

Identifies vaccine candidate with superior cross-strain protection; VS-FCV may paradoxically be ideal immunogen.

## Key Findings

### quoted_fact

- 19 FCV strains isolated China 2021-2022
- HBDL2 strain elicits broadly neutralizing antibodies
- Covers diverse GI and GII genotypes
- Elevated titers against VS-FCV strains

### source_supported_conclusion

- VS-FCV strains can generate superior cross-protective immunity
- HBDL2 is candidate for next-generation FCV vaccine
