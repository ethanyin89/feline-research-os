## Treatment Summary

**LGAL (Standard of Care):**
- Chlorambucil + glucocorticoids (prednisolone)
- Response rates: 69-96%
- Well-tolerated with minimal toxicities
- Median survival: 800-1300 days

**HGAL:**
- CHOP protocol or variants
- Response rates lower
- Median survival: weeks to months

## Molecular Landscape Gaps

The review identifies striking paucity of:
- Genomic studies on clinical material
- Transcriptomic profiling
- Epigenomic/methylome analysis
- Proteomic investigations
- Metabolomic studies

Recent advances in feline genome assembly offer opportunities to address these gaps.

## Inflammatory-Neoplastic Continuum

Key diagnostic challenge:
- IBD and early LGAL can be histologically ambiguous
- Chronic enteropathy may progress to lymphoma
- Immunohistochemistry (CD3, PAX5) aids differentiation
- Clonality testing (PARR) can resolve difficult cases
