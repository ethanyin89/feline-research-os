# Low-Dose Meloxicam in Feline CKD

## Evidence-Depth Caveat

Title-only intake from sheet. Full text needed for dose protocols, renal function monitoring, and safety outcomes. PMID 34923878.

## One-Line Summary

2022 JFMS study evaluating safety and effects of low-dose meloxicam use in cats with chronic kidney disease.

## Why It Matters For Feline CKD

NSAIDs are traditionally avoided in CKD due to renal concerns, but:
1. CKD cats often have comorbid painful conditions (OA, dental)
2. Low-dose protocols may balance analgesia with renal safety
3. Important for quality of life in advanced CKD management
