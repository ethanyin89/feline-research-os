# FCV Vaccine Breakdown Strains Genogrouping in Japan

## Evidence-Depth Caveat

Abstract-weighted extraction. Full text needed for phylogenetic tree and strain details.

## One-Line Summary

Vaccine breakthrough strains cluster by genogroup based on vaccine strain used; F9→GAII, FCV-255→GAI, FC-7→equal.

## Why It Matters For Feline FCV

Explains why single-strain vaccines have incomplete protection; supports multi-strain vaccine strategy.

## Key Findings

### quoted_fact

- 15 breakthrough strains from vaccinated cats analyzed
- 53% GAI, 47% GAII distribution
- F9 vaccine: more GAII breakthrough
- FCV-255 vaccine: more GAI breakthrough
- FC-7 vaccine: equal genogroup distribution

### source_supported_conclusion

- Vaccine-breakthrough genogroup correlates with vaccine strain used
- Neither vaccine strain provides complete cross-genogroup protection
