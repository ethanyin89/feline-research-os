# Large-Scale FCV Genetic Diversity Study

## Evidence-Depth Caveat

Abstract-weighted extraction. Full text needed for phylogenetic trees and sampling methodology.

## One-Line Summary

FCV shows extreme genetic diversity with evolutionary rate of 1.3-2.6 × 10⁻² subs/nt/year, among highest for any virus.

## Why It Matters For Feline FCV

Quantifies FCV evolution rate; explains antigenic diversity and vaccine cross-protection challenges.

## Key Findings

### quoted_fact

- Single diverse genogroup, no subspecies clustering
- Evolutionary rate: 1.3-2.6 × 10⁻² subs/nt/year
- Among highest viral evolution rates known

### source_supported_conclusion

- Extreme genetic diversity in FCV
- Rapid evolution challenges vaccination
