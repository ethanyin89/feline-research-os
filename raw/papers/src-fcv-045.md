# VS-FCV: Cytokine modulation and viral mutants

## Evidence-Depth Caveat

Abstract-weighted extraction. Full text needed for complete cytokine panel data.

## One-Line Summary

VS-FCV causes hemorrhagic fever-like signs through viral invasion coupled with IL-10, TNF-α, and MIP-1α dysregulation; capsid mutations implicated.

## Why It Matters For Feline FCV

Mechanistic understanding of why VS-FCV is so lethal; links viral genetics to immune pathology.

## Key Findings

### quoted_fact

- Mortality up to 67% in healthy adult cats
- 3.8 elevated cytokines on average in affected skin
- IL-10, TNF-α, MIP-1α prominently upregulated
- Capsid gene changes implicated in virulent phenotype

### source_supported_conclusion

- VS-FCV pathogenesis is cytokine-mediated vascular compromise
- Viral evolution in capsid gene drives emerging virulence
