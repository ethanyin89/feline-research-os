# An outbreak of virulent systemic feline calicivirus disease

## Evidence-Depth Caveat

Abstract-weighted extraction. Full text needed for outbreak details, mortality rates, and strain characterization.

## One-Line Summary

Report of a virulent systemic FCV outbreak with high mortality, establishing VS-FCV as a distinct clinical entity.

## Why It Matters For Feline FCV

This is one of the early reports defining virulent systemic FCV (VS-FCV) as a severe, often fatal disease distinct from typical FCV upper respiratory infection.
