# FCV/FHV Prevalence with FeLV/FIV Co-infection (Iran)

## One-Line Summary

100% FCV detection in URTD cats; 87-93% retroviral co-infection; FCV implicated in corneal ulcers.

## Why It Matters For Feline FCV

Documents high retroviral co-infection rates; expands FCV disease spectrum to include corneal disease.

## Key Findings

### quoted_fact

- FCV: 100% in URTD cats
- FIV co-infection: 87%
- FeLV co-infection: 93%
- FHV-1: 43% in sick cats
- Stomatitis: 50%
- FCV alone in 50% of corneal ulcers

### source_supported_conclusion

- Retroviral infections increase FCV disease burden
- FCV variants may cause corneal pathology
