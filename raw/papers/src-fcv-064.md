# Kitten Vaccination Onset of Immunity Study

## Evidence-Depth Caveat

Abstract-weighted extraction. Full text needed for challenge methodology details.

## One-Line Summary

Non-adjuvanted FVRCP vaccine: FPV immunity at 1 week (1 dose); FCV/FHV immunity at 1 week after 2nd dose.

## Why It Matters For Feline FCV

Defines protective immunity timeline for kitten vaccination protocols.

## Key Findings

### quoted_fact

- 8-9 week old SPF kittens challenged
- FPV: protection within 1 week of single dose
- FCV/FHV: protection within 1 week of second dose
- Reduced viral shedding confirms protection

### source_supported_conclusion

- Rapid onset of immunity with non-adjuvanted vaccine
- 3-4 week interval between doses sufficient
