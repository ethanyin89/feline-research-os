# Ozone Antiviral Activity Against FCV

## Evidence-Depth Caveat

Abstract-weighted extraction. Full text needed for complete dose-response data.

## One-Line Summary

Ozone (50 μg/mL, 90 sec) reduces FCV titer by 1.75-2.25 log₁₀ in vitro.

## Why It Matters For Feline FCV

Novel disinfection approach; alternative to chemical disinfectants for environmental control.

## Key Findings

### quoted_fact

- 50 μg/mL O₃/O₂ for 90 seconds: 1.75 log₁₀ reduction (antiviral)
- Up to 2.25 log₁₀ reduction (virucidal, various concentrations)
- Dose-dependent and time-dependent efficacy

### source_supported_conclusion

- Ozone is effective against FCV in controlled conditions
- Both direct virucidal and cellular antiviral effects demonstrated
