# 核心疾病框架

- FIP 当前应被建模成：
  - supportive diagnosis
  - 加上 treatment transformation
- 当前最强的顶层架构是：
  - systemic disease emergence
  - composite recognition
  - mutation utility plus mutation limits
  - disease-form-aware treatment branching

Bottom line:

不要把 FIP 建模成 one-test disease。

---

# 什么主导识别

- clinicopathology
- disease form：
  - effusive
  - non-effusive
  - neurologic extension
- risk context：
  - young age
  - patterned signalment
  - multi-cat exposure

内部含义：

Recognition 是 composite 的，不是 assay-first。

---

# Endpoint hierarchy

## Tier 1

- clinicopathologic pattern recognition
- disease-form-aware composite suspicion

## Tier 2

- mutation-related assays
- acute phase / immunoglobulin support
- older serology history

## Tier 3

- CSF detection for neurologic extension
- treatment follow-up outcomes

---

# Mutation boundary

- mutation studies 很重要
- 但它们在做两种不同工作：
  - explain mechanism
  - support workup
- 它们本身不足以支撑 certainty language

Bottom line:

Positive mutation support 和 mutation limits 必须一起保留。

---

# Treatment transformation

## Distinct layers

- preclinical antiviral foundation
- natural-disease GS-441524 baseline efficacy
- remdesivir-plus-GS package logic
- neurologic rescue / high-complexity treatment
- post-treatment durability

---

# 现代治疗到底改变了什么

- treatment 不再是一个薄薄的 side branch
- GS-441524-era evidence 改变了整个 translational map
- 但 treatment transformation 并没有消除 diagnostic ambiguity

Bottom line:

FIP 现在是一个更难干净诊断、但更可行动治疗的 disease module。
