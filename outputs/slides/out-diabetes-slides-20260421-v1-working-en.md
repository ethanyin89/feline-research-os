# Core Disease Frame

Feline diabetes should currently be modeled as:

`reversible-potential metabolic disease with chronic endocrine and comorbidity constraints`

Do not flatten it into:

- remission protocol only
- diet slogan only
- insulin ladder only
- SGLT2 product story only

---

# Pathogenesis Logic

- type-2-like metabolic disease is central
- obesity and insulin resistance matter
- beta-cell / endocrine constraints remain visible
- secondary endocrine disease can change the case frame
- pancreatitis and DKA context can complicate management

---

# Remission Boundary

Remission is real.

But current evidence does not justify:

- one universal predictor
- one protocol winner
- one treatment-ranking shortcut

Use remission as an outcome branch, not the whole module.

---

# Diet And Obesity

Diet questions must stay separated:

- carbohydrate
- fiber
- protein
- calorie restriction
- palatability / adherence
- stabilization before weight loss

Obesity is a mechanism, recognition, endpoint, and sequencing branch.

---

# Treatment Branches

Current structure:

- diet
- insulin
- SGLT2
- alpha-glucosidase adjunct
- incretin / frontier
- remission-control branch

Rank by role and boundary first, not by enthusiasm.

---

# SGLT2 Boundary

U.S. source control now exists for:

- Bexacat
- Senvelgo

But this only supports:

- U.S. FOI / current-label control
- selected-cat interpretation
- contraindication and monitoring gates

It does not support global status or product superiority.
