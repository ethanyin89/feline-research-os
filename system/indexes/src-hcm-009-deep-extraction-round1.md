## Phase 1: Theme Reconstruction

## Theme: Echocardiography is the lead operational branch for HCM

This paper is one of the clearest reasons the HCM module should remain structure-first. The point is not just that echo can detect disease, but that it defines the phenotype architecture itself.

- 46 domestic cats with spontaneous HCM were characterized by two-dimensional echocardiography
- the study reports a small left-ventricular cavity

The module should not present feline HCM as a single uniform morphologic entity. Variable structural patterns belong inside the main phenotype branch.
