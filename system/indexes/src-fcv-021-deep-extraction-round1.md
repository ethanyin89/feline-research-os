- relation_to_previous: opening branch placement.
- hard_details: the source card preserves FCV detection in 30 of 63 pathogen-positive cats with ocular surface infection and URTD history or symptoms.
- tension_or_surprise: the title is provocative, but the safest role is still extension routing rather than front-door diagnosis.

- hard_details: erosive conjunctivitis is highlighted as an important ocular finding, and oral ulcers were detected in all FCV-infected cats in the preserved source layer.
- tension_or_surprise: the ocular signal strengthens when oral-ulcer context is preserved.
