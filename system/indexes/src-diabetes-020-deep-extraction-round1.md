## Source-Supported Facts

- The abstract says diabetes is the second-most common feline endocrinopathy and affects about 1/200 cats.
- Around 15-25% of diabetic cats are framed as developing diabetes secondarily to growth-hormone-induced insulin resistance.
- HST-associated diabetes is described as challenging to manage, with variable insulin response or high dose needs.
- IGF1 is described as the current diagnostic test of choice, but marginal increases and chronic insulin effects can complicate interpretation.
