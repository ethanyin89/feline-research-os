## Why This Order

- start with one broad HCM review and one broader cardiomyopathy framing source so the module does not collapse too early into one phenotype story
- then lock genetics before severity and biomarker language drift ahead of mechanism
- then lock echocardiographic structure before biomarkers or AI papers start to feel more definitive than phenotype recognition
- then add one modern clinical-and-therapeutic overview and one remodeling-focused paper so treatment and biology both become visible without overcommitting
