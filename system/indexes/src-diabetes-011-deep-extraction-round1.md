## Quoted / Near-Quoted Facts

- The abstract names bexagliflozin and velagliflozin as recently FDA approved for uncomplicated feline diabetes.
- The abstract states that suitable candidates need residual beta-cell function.
- The abstract warns practitioners about serious complications including euglycemic diabetic ketoacidosis.

## Source-Supported Conclusions
