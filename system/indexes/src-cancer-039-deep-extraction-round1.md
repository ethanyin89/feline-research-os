## Phase 2: Theme Reconstruction

### Theme A: Cachexia as Inflammation-Driven

Cancer cachexia is not simple starvation:
- Metabolic dysregulation from tumor and host inflammation
- Cannot be reversed by feeding alone
- Requires anti-inflammatory approaches (omega-3s)
- Involves cytokine-mediated muscle proteolysis

### Theme B: Nutritional Intervention Principles

General principles for cancer nutrition:
1. **Maintain muscle:** High-quality protein
2. **Provide energy:** Fat-based calories (avoid carbohydrate dependence)
3. **Reduce inflammation:** Omega-3 fatty acids
4. **Support function:** Vitamins and micronutrients

### Theme C: Evidence Gaps

The review explicitly notes:
- Further investigation needed
- Current recommendations based on limited evidence
- Need for species-specific (feline) trials
- Dosing and timing not well established
