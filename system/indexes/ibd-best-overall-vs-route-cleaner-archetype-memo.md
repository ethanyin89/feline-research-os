# IBD Best-Overall Vs Route-Cleaner Archetype Memo

- Date: `2026-04-11`
- Scope: comparison layer above the first IBD product-archetype and claim-fit/route-fit memos

This memo exists to answer a narrower operator question:

`if IBD should currently keep two different winners in view, what exactly is the difference between the best overall archetype and the future route-cleaner archetype?`

The regulatory brief already implies this distinction.

This page makes it explicit.

## Core Takeaway

`for IBD, diet-first chronic-enteropathy management is still the best overall archetype, while broad medical-management remains the cleaner future route-fit candidate`

Those are related conclusions.

They are not the same conclusion.

This memo is narrower than:

- [IBD treatment product archetype memo](ibd-treatment-product-archetype-memo.md)
- [IBD claim-fit route-fit boundary memo](ibd-claim-fit-route-fit-boundary-memo.md)

This page answers the tighter comparison question:

`which branch currently wins overall, which branch may later win on route-fit, and why should the vault keep both statements visible at the same time?`

## Current Comparison Read

### Diet-First Chronic-Enteropathy Management

- strongest present practical archetype
- cleaner on current claim-fit than any neighboring branch
- still partly burdened by chronic-enteropathy overlap when route language gets too specific

Current safest read:

`best overall archetype now`

### Broad Immunomodulatory Or Medical Management

- weaker current claim-fit than the diet branch
- more medicine-like shape for later route reasoning
- should not be mistaken for stronger present evidence

Current safest read:

`future route-cleaner archetype, not current best overall archetype`

### Microbiota-Directed, Regenerative, And Assay-Style Branches

- strategically interesting
- still too unstable to compete for either title
- should remain below early route compression

## Why The Two Winners Diverge

The current module is doing two different jobs at once:

- deciding which archetype is most supportable inside the present evidence spine
- deciding which archetype might later fit formal route thinking more cleanly

Those jobs separate because:

- diet-first management is closer to current practical treatment reality
- medical-management is more medicine-like when route logic becomes the main question
- current evidence thickness still favors diet on claim-fit
- future regulatory compression may later favor medicine-style assets on route-fit

## What The Module Can Already Say

- diet-first remains the safest overall first bridge from treatment into regulatory thinking
- medical-management remains the most plausible future route-fit stress test
- keeping both statements visible prevents the module from confusing present evidence strength with future regulatory neatness
- no unstable branch currently deserves either label

## What The Module Should Not Yet Say

- there is no safe claim that medical-management is the current best IBD archetype overall
- there is no safe claim that diet-first is already the cleanest eventual route-fit branch
- there is no safe claim that either branch supports jurisdiction recommendation now
- there is no safe claim that route-cleaner means evidence-superior

## Short Operational Read
