## Why This Order

- start with pathogenesis and a broad clinical overview so the module does not collapse into a diet-only or insulin-only management story
- lock remission boundaries early, because feline diabetes is clinically tempting to overstate as reversible
- bring obesity and endocrine secondary causes near the top, because they change both prognosis and treatment logic
- read diet and treatment studies after the mechanism layer, so interventions are interpreted against disease biology rather than marketing-style endpoints
- use epidemiology after the clinical spine is visible, so prevalence and breed-risk claims do not become the module's main organizing structure
