## What The Module Can Already Say

- treatment should be modeled as a tiered branch, not as one flat intervention list
- diet-first logic is currently the most defensible early practical anchor
- immunomodulatory / medical management is a real second step when dietary response is insufficient, but specific-drug ranking remains thin
- broader therapeutic reviews and stem-cell or regenerative ideas belong in the module, but clearly below feline-primary practical evidence
- treatment language should stay behind diagnostic-boundary and pathology certainty
- the newer [IBD treatment hierarchy feline-primary memo, 2026-04-17](ibd-treatment-hierarchy-feline-primary-memo-20260417.md) should be used when a query needs the defensible treatment ladder rather than only the uncertainty ceiling

## What The Module Should Not Yet Say

- there is no stable final drug ranking yet
- there is no strong idiopathic-IBD-specific drug efficacy hierarchy yet
- exploratory translational branches should not be promoted to routine-ready management
- broad therapeutic-overview material should not be mistaken for feline-primary efficacy
- a practical treatment ladder is not clinical guidance, product-route advice, or submission-grade hierarchy
