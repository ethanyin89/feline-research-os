## Phase 0: Sequential Micro-Analysis

#### Unit 1: The paper directly attacks the old untreatable-FIP frame

- core_claim: this study is a natural-disease efficacy and safety test of GS-441524 in cats with naturally occurring FIP.
- implicit_premise: FIP can no longer be modeled only as a fatal-disease endpoint once direct antiviral efficacy exists in real clinical cases.
- relation_to_previous: opening translational problem shift.
- hard_details: the source is a clinical efficacy and safety study in naturally occurring disease.
- tension_or_surprise: this is not in vitro promise only; it sits at the real clinical-use threshold.

#### Unit 2: The study population is clinically valuable but not universal

- core_claim: the enrolled population spans effusive, dry-to-effusive, and non-effusive disease, but excludes severe neurologic and ocular FIP.
- implicit_premise: case mix defines how far the treatment story can safely be generalized.
- relation_to_previous: moves from importance to sample boundaries.
- hard_details: 31 cats were enrolled; 26 had effusive or dry-to-effusive disease and five had non-effusive disease; severe neurologic and ocular cases were not recruited.
- tension_or_surprise: the paper is stronger than anecdote, but weaker than a universal all-form efficacy statement.

#### Unit 3: The study reports substantial clinical success, but not frictionless cure

- core_claim: the trial reports strong response and multiple long-term healthy survivors, but early deaths, relapse, and retreatment are all part of the story.
- implicit_premise: FIP treatment transformation is real, but it should be modeled as response management rather than instant cure language.
- relation_to_previous: moves from cohort definition to outcomes.
- hard_details: four cats died or were euthanized within 2-5 days; a fifth died after 26 days; 24 cats remained healthy at publication; relapses occurred and some cats responded to retreatment at higher dose.
- tension_or_surprise: the key story is dramatic improvement in outcomes, not perfect linear success.

#### Unit 4: Dose optimization is part of the paper's practical contribution

- core_claim: this is not just a yes/no efficacy paper; it also proposes a practical dosing conclusion.
- implicit_premise: translational value increases when a paper offers a workable regimen rather than only proof of activity.
- relation_to_previous: moves from outcomes to practical protocol.
- hard_details: the abstract identifies 4.0 mg/kg SC every 24 hours for at least 12 weeks as the optimum dosage within the study.
- tension_or_surprise: the paper is partly efficacy evidence and partly early protocol architecture.

#### Unit 5: The paper should become the baseline, not the whole treatment branch

- core_claim: this study is the natural starting anchor for FIP antiviral treatment, but not the endpoint of the branch.
- implicit_premise: later real-world combination protocols and neurologic FIP studies are needed to widen the treatment story.
- relation_to_previous: defines the paper's place in the module.
- hard_details: severe neurologic and ocular FIP were not included.
- tension_or_surprise: the paper is central precisely because it is strong and bounded.

## Phase 1: Theme Reconstruction

## Theme: FIP treatment has crossed from conceptual hope into real clinical-efficacy territory

This paper is one of the clearest reasons the FIP module cannot be written in pre-antiviral language. It places GS-441524 into naturally occurring disease and reports meaningful survival and remission outcomes. That changes the translational center of gravity of the whole disease map.

### Hard Information

- 31 cats enrolled
- 24 remained healthy at publication
- efficacy and safety both explicitly studied

## Theme: The paper defines a baseline treatment branch, not a universal all-form treatment guarantee

The study is strongest when used as the baseline modern treatment anchor. It is weaker when overextended to severe neurologic and ocular disease, because those forms were not recruited.

### Hard Information

- severe neurologic and ocular FIP cases were not recruited
- cohort still included multiple non-neurologic clinical forms

## Theme: Response management and relapse belong inside the treatment architecture

This study should not be compressed into a binary cure claim. Early death, relapse, retreatment, and dose adjustment are all part of the treatment story.

### Hard Information

- four cats died or were euthanized within 2-5 days
- one cat died after 26 days
- relapses occurred
- some cats responded to retreatment at higher dose

## Theme: Practical protocol logic is part of the evidence contribution

The paper matters not only because it reports success, but because it proposes an initial practical treatment architecture around daily SC dosing and minimum duration.

### Hard Information

- initial treatment at 2.0 mg/kg SC daily with increases when indicated
- abstract identifies 4.0 mg/kg SC every 24 hours for at least 12 weeks as optimum dosage within this study
