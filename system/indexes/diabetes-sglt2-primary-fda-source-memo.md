# Diabetes SGLT2 Primary FDA Source Memo

- Date: `2026-04-24`
- Scope: `src-reg-010`, `src-reg-011`, with treatment context from `src-diabetes-011`

This memo exists because the SGLT2 branch now has primary U.S. FDA source cards.

## Core Takeaway

`Bexacat and Senvelgo convert the feline SGLT2 branch into a primary-source-backed U.S. regulatory branch, but the branch is governed by insulin-naive otherwise-healthy candidate selection and ketoacidosis monitoring`

## Primary Source 1: Bexacat

Source card:

- [src-reg-010 Bexacat FDA FOI](../../raw/regulations/src-reg-010-bexacat-fda-foi.md)

Current source-supported facts:

- FDA approved Bexacat under NADA 141-566 on December 8, 2022.
- Bexacat is a bexagliflozin 15 mg oral tablet.
- The indicated population is otherwise healthy cats with diabetes mellitus not previously treated with insulin.
- The product is given once daily to cats weighing at least 3.0 kg.
- In the pivotal 6-month field study, 64/77 cats in the effectiveness analysis were treatment successes at Day 56.
- FDA's FOI summary emphasizes diabetic ketoacidosis and euglycemic diabetic ketoacidosis risk.

## Primary Source 2: Senvelgo

Source card:

- [src-reg-011 Senvelgo FDA FOI](../../raw/regulations/src-reg-011-senvelgo-fda-foi.md)

Current source-supported facts:

- FDA approved Senvelgo under NADA 141-568 on August 10, 2023.
- Senvelgo is a velagliflozin oral solution dosed once daily at 1 mg/kg.
- The indicated population is otherwise healthy cats with diabetes mellitus not previously treated with insulin.
- In the pivotal field study, 175/198 cats in the effectiveness analysis were treatment successes at Day 30.
- Ketonuria and diabetic ketoacidosis drove early removals from the effectiveness analysis.
- FDA's FOI summary emphasizes diabetic ketosis, diabetic ketoacidosis, and euglycemic diabetic ketoacidosis risk.

## What Changed

Before this memo, the regulatory brief could only say:

`src-diabetes-011 reports FDA approval for bexagliflozin and velagliflozin`

Now it can say:

`two primary FDA FOI source cards exist for U.S. feline SGLT2 products`

That is a real status change.

## What Did Not Change

The module still cannot say:

- SGLT2 products are superior to insulin
- oral route is better than injectable treatment
- SGLT2 products are appropriate for complicated diabetes
- U.S. approval implies global availability
- Bexacat and Senvelgo can be ranked without full label and comparator review
