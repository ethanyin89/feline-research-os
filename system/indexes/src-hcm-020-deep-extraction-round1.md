## Phase 1: Theme Reconstruction

## Theme: HCM mechanism should be modeled as active remodeling

### Hard Information

- myocardial remodeling is a key process
- cardiomyocyte disarray, fibrosis, leukocyte infiltration, and vascular dysplasia are described
- inflammatory and profibrotic mediators are increased

## Theme: Macrophages deserve a visible place in the mechanism branch

The strongest reusable addition is not just “fibrosis exists.” It is that macrophage-rich interstitial change appears to help maintain a profibrotic environment.

### Hard Information

- increased interstitial Iba1-positive macrophage-like cells
- increased inflammatory and profibrotic mediator transcription

## Theme: Remodeling links mechanism and translational imagination

This paper does not itself prove a therapy route, but it does justify a future translational branch around remodeling and inflammatory-profibrotic targets.

### Hard Information
