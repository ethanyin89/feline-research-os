## Current Bridge Mechanism Map / 当前桥接机制地图

### Primary Mechanism / 主要机制

**EN**
- `insulin sensitivity decline` is the primary mechanism linking obesity to T2D risk
- insulin sensitivity decreased 52% with obesity in one study (16 research cats)
- glucose effectiveness is also reduced with weight gain
- the mechanism is established at the biological level

**ZH**
- `胰岛素敏感性下降` 是连接肥胖与 T2D 风险的主要机制
- 在一项研究中（16 只研究猫）肥胖导致胰岛素敏感性下降 52%
- 葡萄糖有效性也随体重增加而降低
- 该机制在生物学层面已确立

### Individual Susceptibility / 个体易感性

**EN**
- not all obese cats develop glucose intolerance
- cats with lower lean-state insulin sensitivity are at greater risk
- cats with lower lean-state glucose effectiveness are at greater risk
- predisposition matters; the obesity-diabetes bridge is not deterministic

**ZH**
- 并非所有肥胖猫都会发展出葡萄糖不耐受
- 瘦体状态下胰岛素敏感性较低的猫风险更高
- 瘦体状态下葡萄糖有效性较低的猫风险更高
- 易感性很重要；肥胖-糖尿病桥接不是决定性的

### Study Design Context / 研究设计背景

**EN**
- primary mechanism source: 16 research cats, 10-month induced weight gain
- marked weight increase (44% average)
- controlled conditions may not match household cats
- strong within-subject design for mechanism but not for extrapolation to screening rules

**ZH**
- 主要机制来源：16 只研究猫，10 个月诱导增重
- 明显的体重增加（平均 44%）
- 受控条件可能与家庭猫不匹配
- 对机制来说是强的被试内设计，但不适用于外推到筛查规则

### Association Visibility / 关联可见性

**EN**
- obesity is associated with type 2 diabetes and insulin resistance
- this association is documented in multiple reviews
- the bridge direction matters: this page views from obesity side toward diabetes

**ZH**
- 肥胖与 2 型糖尿病和胰岛素抵抗相关
- 这种关联在多篇综述中有记录
- 桥接方向很重要：这页从肥胖侧向糖尿病方向看
