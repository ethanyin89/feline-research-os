## Current Mechanism Map / 当前机制地图

### Risk Factor Framework / 风险因素框架

**EN**
- `intrinsic factors` (breed, age, sex, reproductive status) are one half of the risk map
- `extrinsic factors` (lifestyle, diet, feeding patterns, owner factors) are the other half
- neither category alone explains obesity; both must be modeled together
- modern domestic cat lifestyle differs substantially from evolutionary context

**ZH**
- `内在因素` (品种、年龄、性别、绝育状态) 是风险地图的一半
- `外在因素` (生活方式、饮食、喂养模式、主人因素) 是另一半
- 单独任何一类都不能解释肥胖；两者必须一起建模
- 现代家猫生活方式与进化语境有实质差异

### Prevalence Layer / 患病率层

**EN**
- obesity prevalence ranges from 11.5% to 63% depending on population and geography
- this makes obesity the most common nutritional disorder in pet cats
- specific prevalence for any single population requires population-specific data
- framing as "most common nutritional disorder" is source-supported

**ZH**
- 肥胖患病率从 11.5% 到 63% 不等，取决于人群和地理位置
- 这使肥胖成为宠物猫最常见的营养性疾病
- 任何单一人群的具体患病率需要该人群的特定数据
- "最常见营养性疾病"的框架有来源支撑

### Associated Conditions Network / 相关疾病网络

**EN**
- obesity is associated with multiple pathology categories, not a single downstream disease
- mechanism types span metabolic (T2D, insulin resistance, hepatic lipidosis), mechanical (musculoskeletal, lameness), and mixed (skin, urinary, kidney)
- association visibility is established; specific causal pathways are partially characterized

**ZH**
- 肥胖与多个病理类别相关，而非单一下游疾病
- 机制类型涵盖代谢性 (T2D、胰岛素抵抗、肝脂肪变性)、机械性 (肌肉骨骼、跛行) 和混合性 (皮肤、泌尿、肾脏)
- 关联可见性已建立；具体因果通路已部分表征

### Diabetes Bridge Layer / 糖尿病桥接层

**EN**
- `insulin sensitivity decline` is the primary mechanism linking obesity to T2D risk
- insulin sensitivity decreases in cats as body weight increases
- this relationship is established at the mechanism level
- not all obese cats become diabetic; individual susceptibility matters

**ZH**
- `胰岛素敏感性下降` 是连接肥胖与 T2D 风险的主要机制
- 猫的胰岛素敏感性随体重增加而降低
- 这种关系在机制层面已确立
- 并非所有肥胖猫都会患糖尿病；个体易感性很重要

### Prevention Priority Layer / 预防优先层

**EN**
- prevention is framed as preferable to treatment
- treatment is slow, often unsuccessful, and not without consequences
- target population: post-gonadectomy kittens aged 5-12 months
- the post-neuter period is the highest-risk window for weight gain

**ZH**
- 预防被框定为优于治疗
- 治疗缓慢、常常不成功、且有代价
- 目标人群：绝育后 5-12 月龄的幼猫
- 绝育后阶段是体重增加风险最高的窗口期

### Assessment Branch / 评估分支

**EN**
- body condition evaluation is essential for determining ideal body weight
- multiple established assessment techniques exist
- specific scoring thresholds need full-text verification from source cards

**ZH**
