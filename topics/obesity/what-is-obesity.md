# 什么是猫肥胖？ / What is Feline Obesity?

## 简单回答 / Simple Answer

**猫肥胖是指猫的体重明显超过健康体重，身体积累过多脂肪的状态。**

Feline obesity is when a cat carries significantly more body weight than is healthy, with excess fat accumulation.

[source_supported_conclusion: src-obesity-001]

## 有多常见？ / How Common Is It?

猫肥胖是宠物猫最常见的营养问题。根据不同地区和研究人群，**11.5% 到 63%** 的猫存在超重或肥胖问题。

Obesity is the most common nutritional disorder in pet cats. Depending on the population and geography, **11.5% to 63%** of cats are overweight or obese.

[quoted_fact: src-obesity-001]

## 为什么会发生？ / Why Does It Happen?

猫变胖的原因可以分为两类：

Cats become obese due to two categories of factors:

### 猫自身因素 / Cat-Related Factors

| 因素 | 说明 |
|------|------|
| 绝育 | 绝育后代谢改变，更容易增重 |
| 年龄 | 不同年龄风险不同 |
| 品种 | 某些品种更容易发胖 |

| Factor | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Neutering | Metabolic changes after neutering increase weight gain risk |
| Age | Risk varies by life stage |
| Breed | Some breeds are predisposed |

### 生活环境因素 / Lifestyle Factors

| 因素 | 说明 |
|------|------|
| 室内久坐 | 活动量低于户外/狩猎猫 |
| 自由采食 | 全天候提供食物 vs 定时定量喂养 |
| 加工食品 | 现代猫粮与猫的狩猎本能饮食不同 |
| 主人喂养习惯 | 用食物表达爱意 |

| Factor | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Indoor sedentary life | Low activity compared to outdoor/hunting cats |
| Free feeding | Food available all day vs portion-controlled meals |
| Processed foods | Modern cat food differs from evolutionary hunting diet |
| Owner feeding habits | Using food to show affection |

## 肥胖有什么危害？ / What Are the Health Risks?

肥胖会增加多种疾病的风险：

Obesity increases the risk of several health problems:

| 健康问题 | Health Problem |
|----------|----------------|
| 糖尿病（2型） | Type 2 diabetes |
| 关节问题、跛行 | Joint problems, lameness |
| 皮肤问题 | Skin disorders |
| 泌尿系统疾病 | Urinary tract disease |
| 肝脏脂肪堆积 | Hepatic lipidosis |

**关键机制：** 猫的体重增加时，胰岛素敏感性会下降，这是肥胖导致糖尿病的主要原因。

**Key mechanism:** As a cat's body weight increases, insulin sensitivity decreases. This is the primary link between obesity and diabetes.

[source_supported_conclusion: src-obesity-001, src-obesity-004, src-obesity-008]

## 如何判断猫是否肥胖？ / How Do I Know If My Cat Is Obese?

**身体状况评估（Body Condition Scoring）** 是判断猫是否肥胖的基础方法。

Body condition assessment is the foundation for recognizing obesity.

简单检查：
- 能否感觉到肋骨？健康体重的猫应该能轻易摸到肋骨
- 俯视猫的身体，是否有腰线？
- 侧面看，腹部是否下垂？

Simple checks:
- Can you feel the ribs? A healthy-weight cat's ribs should be easy to feel
- Looking from above, does your cat have a waist?
- From the side, does the belly hang down?

**注意：** 主人常常低估自己猫的体重状况。如果不确定，请咨询兽医。

**Note:** Owners often underestimate their cat's weight status. If unsure, consult a veterinarian.

[source_supported_conclusion: src-obesity-001]

## 最佳预防时机 / Best Time for Prevention

**绝育后 5-12 个月龄的幼猫** 是预防肥胖的关键时期。

**Post-neutering kittens aged 5-12 months** are the primary target population for obesity prevention.

这个时期代谢变化与生长完成交汇，是最容易发胖的窗口期。预防比治疗更有效，因为减肥治疗过程缓慢、常常失败、且有副作用。

This window is when metabolic changes from neutering intersect with growth completion. Prevention is more effective than treatment because weight loss is slow, often unsuccessful, and not without consequences.

[quoted_fact: src-obesity-005]

## 下一步 / Next Step

**如果你担心猫可能超重：**

1. 使用体况评分（BCS）评估：理想是5/9，超过7/9需要关注
2. 安排兽医检查，排除甲状腺问题等医学原因
3. 不要突然大幅减少猫的食物量

**If you're concerned your cat may be overweight:**

1. Use body condition score (BCS): ideal is 5/9, above 7/9 needs attention
2. Schedule a vet exam to rule out medical causes like thyroid issues
3. Do not suddenly reduce your cat's food intake dramatically

**请注意：** 减肥需要缓慢进行。过快减重可能导致脂肪肝（肝脂质沉积症），这是一种严重的疾病。

**Note:** Weight loss must be gradual. Losing weight too fast can cause fatty liver disease (hepatic lipidosis), which is a serious condition.
