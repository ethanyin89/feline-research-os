## Current Risk Factor Map / 当前风险因素地图

### Intrinsic Factors / 内在因素

**EN**
- `reproductive status` (neutering) is the strongest identified modifiable intrinsic factor
- post-gonadectomy kittens aged 5-12 months are the primary target population
- breed, age, and sex also contribute but effect sizes are not ranked in current sources
- intrinsic factors represent the animal-specific side of the risk map

**ZH**
- `绝育状态` 是已识别的最强可改变内在因素
- 绝育后 5-12 月龄的幼猫是主要目标人群
- 品种、年龄、性别也有贡献，但当前来源未排序效应量
- 内在因素代表风险地图中动物特定的一侧

### Extrinsic Factors / 外在因素

**EN**
- `sedentary indoor lifestyle` differs substantially from evolutionary hunting context
- feeding pattern changes (ad-lib vs hunting) contribute to energy imbalance
- commercial processed foods represent a shift from raw/hunting diet
- owner behavior (feeding as bonding, perception gap) mediates multiple risk factors

**ZH**
- `久坐室内生活方式` 与进化狩猎语境有实质差异
- 喂养模式变化（自由采食 vs 狩猎）导致能量失衡
- 商业加工食品代表从生食/狩猎饮食的转变
- 主人行为（喂食作为联结、认知差距）介导多个风险因素

### Owner Perception Gap / 主人认知差距

**EN**
- owners often underestimate their cat's body condition
- this underestimation delays recognition and intervention
- owner education about body condition assessment is part of the recognition pathway
- the perception gap is itself a risk factor, not just a communication problem

**ZH**
- 主人经常低估猫的体况
- 这种低估延迟了识别和干预
- 对主人进行体况评估教育是识别路径的一部分
- 认知差距本身就是风险因素，而不仅仅是沟通问题

### Body Condition Assessment / 体况评估

**EN**
- body condition evaluation is essential for recognizing obesity
- multiple established techniques exist
- specific scoring thresholds need full-text verification
- assessment should be routine, not triggered only by obvious obesity

**ZH**
- 体况评估对识别肥胖至关重要
- 存在多种已确立的技术
- 具体评分阈值需要全文核实
- 评估应该是常规的，而非仅在明显肥胖时触发
