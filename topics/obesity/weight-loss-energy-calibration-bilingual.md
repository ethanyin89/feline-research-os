## 1. Energy Calculation Overestimation / 能量计算的高估问题

* **EN:** Standard maintenance energy equations (e.g., NRC guidelines) frequently overestimate actual requirements for neutered, overweight cats.
* **ZH:** 标准维持能量公式（如 NRC 指南）经常高估绝育后超重猫的实际能量需求。
* **EN:** The daily energy requirement for successful weight loss is approximately **14% lower** than standard calculations.
* **ZH:** 成功减重的每日能量需求比标准计算值**低大约 14%**。
* **EN:** Recommend targeting **$50 \text{ to } 60 \text{ kcal/kg}$ of target body weight daily**.
* **ZH:** 推荐的减重能量目标为：每日 **$50 \text{ 至 } 60 \text{ kcal/kg}$ 目标体重**。

---

## 2. Muscle Mass Preservation / 肌肉量保护

* **EN:** Lean muscle mass loss accelerates between weeks 12 and 16 of continuous calorie restriction.
* **ZH:** 在连续限制卡路里的第 12 至 16 周之间，肌肉量流失速度会加快。
* **EN:** To preserve lean mass, diets must contain at least **40% metabolizable energy from protein**.
* **ZH:** 为保护肌肉，减重处方粮的**蛋白质能量占比必须至少达到 40%**。

---

## 3. Microbiome and Adiponectin / 菌群与脂联素

* **EN:** Caloric restriction produces beneficial gut microbiome shifts: Actinobacteria increases and Bacteroidetes decreases.
* **ZH:** 卡路里限制会带来有益的肠道菌群改变：放线菌属（Actinobacteria）增加，拟杆菌属（Bacteroidetes）减少。
* **EN:** Reduced fat mass restores secretory levels of the insulin-sensitizing hormone **adiponectin**.
* **ZH:** 脂肪质量的减少可以恢复胰岛素增敏激素——**脂联素（Adiponectin）**的正常分泌水平。
