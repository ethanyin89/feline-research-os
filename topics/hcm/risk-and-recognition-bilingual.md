## Current Recognition Architecture / 当前识别架构

### Entry Frame / 进入框架

**EN**
- recognition should begin from cardiomyopathy-aware clinical suspicion, not from a single biomarker
- HCM suspicion is not the same thing as confirmed phenotype
- broader feline cardiomyopathy context should remain visible

**ZH**
- 识别起点应是带有 cardiomyopathy 意识的临床怀疑，而不是单一 biomarker
- HCM suspicion 不等于 confirmed phenotype
- 更宽的 feline cardiomyopathy context 应继续保持可见

### Structural Confirmation Layer / 结构确认层

**EN**
- structural phenotype confirmation remains the lead branch
- echocardiography and gross morphometry should remain above screening augmentation
- mild-to-moderate thickening should stay in an exclusion-aware zone rather than automatic certainty

**ZH**
- 结构表型确认仍然是主导分支
- echocardiography 和 gross morphometry 应继续放在 screening augmentation 之上
- mild-to-moderate thickening 应继续保留在带排除意识的区域，而不是自动确诊

### Screening-Augmentation Layer / 筛查增强层

**EN**
- NT-proBNP and AI-like branches belong here
- frontier biomarkers should also stay here rather than displacing confirmation
- they can support routing or suspicion-building
- they should not replace structure-first confirmation

**ZH**
- NT-proBNP 和 AI 类分支应放在这里
- frontier biomarkers 也应留在这里，而不是去挤掉 confirmation 层
- 它们可以帮助 routing 或 suspicion-building
- 它们不应替代 structure-first confirmation

### Genotype- And Age-Aware Interpretation / 带 genotype 和年龄意识的解释层

**EN**
- age-related penetrance matters
- genotype pressure can modify suspicion and severity interpretation
- mutation pressure should not be promoted into stand-alone diagnostic certainty

**ZH**
- age-related penetrance 很重要
- genotype pressure 可以修正怀疑度和严重度解释
- mutation pressure 不应被提升成 standalone diagnostic certainty

### Boundary-Aware Layer / 边界意识层

**EN**
- HCM should remain inside a broader cardiomyopathy frame
- recognition should not let HCM absorb all feline myocardial disease
- non-HCM cardiomyopathy context matters as an adjacent boundary

**ZH**
- HCM 应继续放在更宽的 cardiomyopathy 框架内理解
- 识别层不应让 HCM 吞掉全部 feline myocardial disease
- 非 HCM cardiomyopathy 的语境应作为相邻边界保留

## What We Can Defend / 当前可以稳妥主张的内容

**EN**
- HCM recognition is more about workup architecture than about one-marker compression
- structural phenotype should lead before biomarkers and AI
- frontier augmentation should stay below confirmation even when it becomes more interesting
- age and genotype can sharpen interpretation without replacing confirmation
- boundary awareness belongs inside recognition from the beginning

**ZH**
- HCM 的识别更像 workup architecture，而不是 one-marker compression
- 在 biomarkers 和 AI 之前，结构表型应继续主导
- 即使 frontier augmentation 变得更有趣，它也应继续放在 confirmation 之下
- 年龄和 genotype 可以加深解释，但不能替代确认
- 边界意识应从 recognition 一开始就放进去

## What We Should Not Overstate / 当前不应说过头的内容

- no current recognition layer should be rewritten as one-test diagnosis
- NT-proBNP is not a reliable mild-disease screen
- mutation status is not phenotype confirmation
- AI is not routine first-wave authority

**ZH**
- 当前没有任何识别层应被改写成 one-test diagnosis
