## Strongest Current Conclusions / 当前最稳的结论

### Core Backbone / 核心骨架

**EN**
- Feline HCM is best modeled as a structure-first disease module.
- The first major compression problem is not one biomarker or one therapy, but how to separate `phenotype definition`, `screening augmentation`, `genotype pressure`, and `remodeling depth`.
- The strongest current top-layer architecture is:
  - structural phenotype confirmation
  - bounded screening augmentation
  - dosage-sensitive genotype pressure
  - remodeling beneath phenotype
  - treatment skepticism alongside treatment reality

**ZH**
- 猫 HCM 最适合被建模成一个以结构表型为先的 disease module。
- 当前第一位的压缩问题不是某一个 biomarker 或某一种治疗，而是如何分开 `phenotype definition`、`screening augmentation`、`genotype pressure` 和 `remodeling depth`。
- 当前最稳的顶层架构是：
  - 结构表型确认
  - 有边界的 screening augmentation
  - 对 dosage 敏感的 genotype pressure
  - 位于 phenotype 之下的 remodeling
  - 与治疗现实并存的 treatment skepticism

### Recognition And Endpoints / 识别与终点

**EN**
- Echocardiography and gross morphometry currently lead the operational branch.
- `NT-proBNP` is better read as a severe-disease flag than as a reliable mild-disease screen.
- `troponin` currently belongs more naturally to injury or severity interpretation than to primary phenotype definition.
- novel biomarkers and AI are now clearer as augmentation/frontier branches rather than routine authority.
- the biomarker branch is now clearer by use case than by one flat marker ranking.

**ZH**
- echocardiography 和 gross morphometry 目前仍然主导 operational branch。
- `NT-proBNP` 目前更适合被理解成 severe-disease flag，而不是可靠的 mild-disease screening 工具。
- `troponin` 目前更自然地属于 injury 或 severity interpretation，而不是 primary phenotype definition。
- novel biomarkers 和 AI 现在更清楚地属于 augmentation/frontier 分支，而不是 routine authority。
- biomarker 分支现在更适合按 use case 理解，而不是按一个平面 marker ranking 理解。

### Genetics And Severity / 遗传与严重度

**EN**
- the genetics branch should not be flattened into mutation present versus absent
- mutation dosage and age-related penetrance already matter in the current HCM map
- genotype should deepen recognition and severity interpretation, not replace structure

**ZH**
- genetics 分支不应被压成 mutation present versus absent 的二元故事
- mutation dosage 和 age-related penetrance 已经在当前 HCM 地图里有实际意义
- genotype 应该加深识别和严重度解释，而不是替代结构表型

### Remodeling And Late Disease / Remodeling 与晚期疾病

**EN**
- remodeling is not a passive after-effect
- fibrosis is now one of the clearest bridge variables between mechanism and severity depth
- end-stage HCM should be read as a remodeled phenotype, not simply as “more hypertrophy”

**ZH**
- remodeling 不是被动后果
- fibrosis 现在已经是 mechanism 和 severity depth 之间最清楚的桥接变量之一
- end-stage HCM 应该被理解成一种 remodeled phenotype，而不只是“更重的 hypertrophy”

### Treatment / 治疗层

**EN**
- the current treatment branch is real, but not flat
- the safest current frame is `bounded management + selective targeted frontier`
- frontier should now be split into targeted therapy, biomarker frontier, and AI augmentation rather than one innovation bucket
- prescribing behavior and evidence strength should remain separate in the compiled layer

**ZH**
- 当前治疗分支是真实存在的，但不是平面的
- 当前最稳的框架是 `bounded management + selective targeted frontier`
- frontier 现在更适合分成 targeted therapy、biomarker frontier 和 AI augmentation，而不是一个创新大桶
- prescribing behavior 和 evidence strength 在编译层里应继续保持分开

### Boundary Handling / 边界处理

- non-HCM cardiomyopathies belong to the extension boundary, not inside the HCM core spine

**ZH**
