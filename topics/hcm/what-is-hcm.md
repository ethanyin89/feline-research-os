# 什么是猫肥厚性心肌病？ / What is Feline HCM?

## Quantified Claim Traceability

| Claim | Source IDs | Boundary |

## 简单回答 / Simple Answer

**猫肥厚性心肌病（HCM）是猫最常见的心脏病，表现为心肌异常增厚。**

心肌增厚会导致心脏腔室变小，影响心脏正常泵血功能。这是一种进行性疾病，可能导致心力衰竭或其他严重并发症。

Feline hypertrophic cardiomyopathy (HCM) is the most common heart disease in cats, characterized by abnormal thickening of the heart muscle.

The thickened muscle causes the heart chambers to become smaller, affecting the heart's ability to pump blood normally. It's a progressive disease that can lead to heart failure or other serious complications.

[source_supported_conclusion: src-hcm-001, src-hcm-002]

## 有多常见？ / How Common Is It?

HCM 是猫最常见的心脏病，估计影响约 **10-15%** 的猫群体。

HCM is the most common heart disease in cats, estimated to affect about **10-15%** of the cat population.

- 任何品种都可能发生
- 某些品种风险更高（缅因库恩猫、布偶猫等）
- 公猫发病率可能略高
- 通常在中年发现，但可在任何年龄出现

- Can occur in any breed
- Some breeds at higher risk (Maine Coon, Ragdoll, etc.)
- Male cats may have slightly higher incidence
- Usually discovered in middle age, but can appear at any age

[quoted_fact: src-hcm-003]

## 有什么症状？ / What Are the Signs?

HCM 的症状差异很大，从无症状到危及生命：

HCM symptoms vary widely, from no symptoms to life-threatening:

### 早期可能无症状 / May Be Asymptomatic Early

很多 HCM 猫在早期没有明显症状，可能在常规检查时偶然发现心脏杂音。

Many cats with HCM show no obvious symptoms early on and may be discovered incidentally when a heart murmur is detected during routine examination.

### 可能出现的症状 / Possible Signs

| 症状 | Sign |
|------|------|
| 呼吸困难/急促 | Difficulty breathing / rapid breathing |
| 精神变差 | Lethargy |
| 食欲下降 | Decreased appetite |
| 运动不耐受 | Exercise intolerance |
| 后腿瘫痪（血栓） | Hind leg paralysis (thromboembolism) |
| 突然死亡 | Sudden death |

### 危险信号 / Emergency Signs

- **呼吸困难**（张口呼吸、腹式呼吸）
- **后腿突然瘫痪**、冰凉、疼痛
- 这些是急症，需立即就医

- **Breathing difficulty** (open-mouth breathing, abdominal breathing)
- **Sudden hind leg paralysis**, cold, painful legs
- These are emergencies requiring immediate veterinary care

[source_supported_conclusion: src-hcm-001]

## 如何诊断？ / How Is It Diagnosed?

**超声心动图（心脏彩超）** 是诊断 HCM 的金标准。

**Echocardiography (cardiac ultrasound)** is the gold standard for diagnosing HCM.

| 检查方法 | 作用 |
|----------|------|
| 听诊 | 可能发现心脏杂音或心律不齐 |
| 心脏超声 | 测量心肌厚度，评估心脏功能 |
| 血压测量 | 排除高血压导致的继发性心肌肥厚 |
| 甲状腺检查 | 排除甲亢（老年猫） |
| 心脏生物标志物 | NT-proBNP 等可作为筛查辅助 |
| 心电图 | 评估心律 |

| Test | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| Auscultation | May detect heart murmur or arrhythmia |
| Cardiac ultrasound | Measure muscle thickness, assess heart function |
| Blood pressure | Rule out secondary hypertrophy from hypertension |
| Thyroid test | Rule out hyperthyroidism (older cats) |
| Cardiac biomarkers | NT-proBNP can aid screening |
| ECG | Assess heart rhythm |

**诊断定义：** 心室壁厚度 ≥6mm（在排除其他原因后）通常被认为是 HCM。

**Diagnostic definition:** Ventricular wall thickness ≥6mm (after ruling out other causes) is generally considered HCM.

[source_supported_conclusion: src-hcm-009, src-hcm-024]

## 某些品种风险更高 / Some Breeds at Higher Risk

已知某些品种携带与 HCM 相关的基因突变：

Some breeds are known to carry genetic mutations associated with HCM:

| 品种 | Breed |
|------|-------|
| 缅因库恩猫 | Maine Coon |
| 布偶猫 | Ragdoll |
| 英国短毛猫 | British Shorthair |
| 波斯猫 | Persian |

**注意：** 基因检测可用于某些品种，但阴性结果不能完全排除 HCM。

**Note:** Genetic testing is available for some breeds, but a negative result does not completely rule out HCM.

[source_supported_conclusion: src-hcm-001]

## 可以治疗吗？ / Can It Be Treated?

**HCM 目前无法治愈，但可以管理。** 治疗目标是减缓疾病进展和预防并发症。

**HCM cannot be cured, but it can be managed.** Treatment goals are to slow disease progression and prevent complications.

### 可能的治疗措施 / Possible Treatments

| 措施 | 目的 |
|------|------|
| 药物治疗 | 控制心率、减轻心脏负担 |
| 抗血栓药物 | 预防血栓形成 |
| 利尿剂 | 如有心衰迹象 |
| 定期监测 | 跟踪疾病进展 |

| Treatment | Purpose |
|-----------|---------|
| Medications | Control heart rate, reduce cardiac workload |
| Antithrombotic drugs | Prevent blood clot formation |
| Diuretics | If signs of heart failure |
| Regular monitoring | Track disease progression |

**重要：** 治疗方案因个体而异。目前没有明确证据证明哪种治疗最有效。

**Important:** Treatment plans vary by individual. There is currently no definitive evidence proving which treatment is most effective.

[source_supported_conclusion: src-hcm-001]

## 下一步 / Next Step

**如果你担心猫可能有心脏病：**

1. 安排心脏超声检查（确诊的唯一方法）
2. 记录任何异常呼吸或活动减少
3. 不要给猫施加过大压力（如剧烈运动）

**If you're concerned your cat may have heart disease:**

1. Schedule an echocardiogram (the only way to confirm diagnosis)
2. Document any abnormal breathing or reduced activity
3. Avoid putting stress on your cat (e.g., strenuous exercise)

**请注意：** 许多HCM猫没有明显症状。定期筛查对高风险品种尤为重要。

**Note:** Many HCM cats have no obvious symptoms. Regular screening is especially important for high-risk breeds.
