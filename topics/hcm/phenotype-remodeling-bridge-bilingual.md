## Current Bridge Architecture / 当前桥接架构

### Structural Phenotype Front Door / 结构表型前门

**EN**
- echocardiographic structure is still the lead operational entry point
- the phenotype is heterogeneous rather than one uniform hypertrophy pattern
- small-cavity and variable hypertrophy patterns matter before deeper tissue interpretation

**ZH**
- echocardiographic structure 仍然是主导性的 operational entry point
- 这个 phenotype 是异质性的，而不是一种统一的 hypertrophy pattern
- small-cavity 和 variable hypertrophy patterns 在更深 tissue interpretation 之前就已经重要

### Remodeling Beneath Phenotype / phenotype 之下的 remodeling

**EN**
- remodeling is not a passive after-effect
- fibrosis, macrophage-rich interstitial change, and profibrotic signaling deepen the mechanism branch
- the visible phenotype should be read as the surface of deeper biologic remodeling

**ZH**
- remodeling 不是被动后果
- fibrosis、macrophage-rich interstitial change 和 profibrotic signaling 会加深机制分支
- 可见 phenotype 应被理解成更深 biologic remodeling 的表面

### End-Stage Remodeled Phenotype / 晚期重构表型

**EN**
- end-stage HCM is not just “more hypertrophy”
- fibrosis, chamber dilation, and vascular change matter in this layer
- pathology depth helps explain how phenotype evolves across severity

**ZH**
- end-stage HCM 不只是“更重的 hypertrophy”
- fibrosis、chamber dilation 和 vascular change 在这一层都重要
- pathology depth 有助于解释 phenotype 如何随严重度演化

## What We Can Defend / 当前可以稳妥主张的内容

**EN**
- structural phenotype still leads recognition
- remodeling deepens phenotype interpretation rather than replacing it
- end-stage HCM is a remodeled phenotype, not just a thicker-heart state
- fibrosis is one of the clearest bridge variables between mechanism and severity architecture

**ZH**
- 结构表型仍然主导识别层
- remodeling 会加深 phenotype interpretation，而不是替代 phenotype
- end-stage HCM 是重构后的 phenotype，而不只是“更厚的心脏”
- fibrosis 是 mechanism 与 severity architecture 之间最清楚的桥接变量之一

## What We Should Not Overstate / 当前不应说过头的内容

**EN**
- remodeling should not outrank phenotype in first-pass diagnosis
- no single remodeling story explains all HCM heterogeneity
- pathology depth should not be mistaken for routine first-wave authority

**ZH**
- 在 first-pass diagnosis 里，remodeling 不应压过 phenotype
- 没有单一 remodeling story 能解释全部 HCM 异质性
- pathology depth 不应被误当成 routine first-wave authority
