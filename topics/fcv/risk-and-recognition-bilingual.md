# 猫杯状病毒 (FCV) 风险与识别 / Feline FCV Risk And Recognition

## 核心要点 / Core Takeaway

FCV 识别目前应保持复合性：口腔和上呼吸道疾病是核心，但健康排毒、共病原、眼部疾病和可能的肠道扩展防止单一症状或单一测试诊断。

FCV recognition should stay composite: oral and upper-respiratory disease are core, but healthy shedding, co-pathogens, ocular disease, and enteric extension prevent one-symptom or one-test diagnosis.

## 当前安全解读 / Current Safe Read

- FCV 可疑体征不等于 FCV 确认 / FCV-suspect signs ≠ FCV confirmation
- 健康猫可排毒 FCV / Healthy cats can shed FCV
- 共病原在症状性呼吸道表现中很重要 / Co-pathogens matter in respiratory presentations
- 眼部和肠道分支应保持为扩展分支 / Ocular and enteric branches should remain extension branches

## 工作识别顺序 / Working Recognition Order

1. 当口腔溃疡和上呼吸道疾病聚集时怀疑 FCV / Suspect FCV when oral ulceration and upper-respiratory disease cluster
2. 在过度归因因果性之前保持携带和健康排毒在视野中 / Keep carriage and healthy shedding in view before overcalling causality
3. 在呼吸道病例中积极保留共病原结构 / Actively preserve co-pathogen structure in respiratory cases
4. 将眼部和肠道信号作为扩展分支路由，而非一线证明 / Route ocular and enteric signals as extension branches

## 当前识别架构 / Current Recognition Architecture

- `src-fcv-004`, `src-fcv-009`: 支持经典 FCV 识别核心 — 口腔、上呼吸道和更广综合征意识
- `src-fcv-009`: RT-PCR 是最佳检测工具，但阴性结果不排除感染，健康猫可能检测阳性
- `src-fcv-006`: 过度诊断控制关键论文，直接比较症状和健康猫
- `src-fcv-005`: 地理特异性流行病学压力，毒株分布和疫苗适配可能因地区而异
- `src-fcv-021`: 保持眼表面疾病在 FCV 识别中可见
- `src-fcv-020`: 保持肠道关联在 FCV 识别中可见

## 护栏 / Guardrail

不要让 FCV 识别崩塌为"看到溃疡即确认"。健康排毒、共病原结构和 PCR 解释警示必须保持可见。

Do not let FCV recognition collapse into "see ulcer, confirm FCV." Healthy shedding, co-pathogen structure, and PCR interpretation caution must stay visible.
