## Current State

The FCV seed corpus does not yet include a dedicated regulator-label corpus. Current vaccine and control statements are still paper-led.

## Guardrail

Do not turn FCV vaccine or treatment papers into jurisdiction-specific approval claims. This page currently exists to hold that boundary, not to answer product-registration questions.

## Current Safe Read

- FCV has vaccine and control literature pressure
- the module does not yet have FDA / EMA / VMD-style FCV regulatory source cards
- product or label-level claims should remain out of scope until such a corpus exists

## What This Page Can Safely Do

- hold the line between `vaccine/control evidence` and `regulatory/label evidence`
- prevent paper-led protection language from drifting into approval-status language
- mark current-source gaps before someone tries to write jurisdiction-specific product claims

## What This Page Cannot Yet Do

- answer whether a given FCV vaccine has current approval in a specific jurisdiction
- compare label claims across marketed products
- state current regulator-recognized indications, age gates, booster schedules, or claim wording

## Guardrail

FCV now has enough vaccine literature to support a control-strategy discussion, but still not enough official-source material to support jurisdiction-specific regulatory prose. The next regulatory upgrade is not another vaccine paper; it is a real label / official-guidance source pack.
