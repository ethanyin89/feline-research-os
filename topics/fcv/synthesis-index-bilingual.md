## 当前综合主线 / Current Synthesis Spine

- FCV 当前围绕"综述/指南"、"疫苗/免疫"、"流行病学/识别"、"治疗"和"扩展/组织趋向性"分支组织
- 指南和综述来源应控制疾病框架和分支命名
- 疫苗证据是最厚的分支，但需仔细分离：保护广度、挑战模型、平台设计、细胞 vs 体液免疫、疫苗失败解释和持久性不可互换
- VS-FCV（毒力系统性 FCV）代表独特临床实体，不应与常规上呼吸道感染混淆
- 治疗作为真实分支存在，但预防/疫苗接种仍是主要控制策略
- 组织趋向性和扩展分支将疾病图扩展到经典口腔/呼吸表现之外

FCV currently organizes around broad review/guidance, vaccine/immunity, epidemiology/recognition, therapy, and extension/tissue tropism branches. Vaccine evidence is the thickest branch but requires careful separation across breadth, challenge, platform design, immunity type, vaccine-failure interpretation, and persistence. VS-FCV represents a distinct clinical entity. Therapy exists but prevention/vaccination remains primary.

## 当前工作假设 / Current Working Hypothesis

FCV 应保持为"疫苗/免疫复杂性"、"VS-FCV 区分"、"携带者持久性"和"治疗局限性"都在顶层可见的疾病模块，而不是压缩为简单的"疫苗接种解决一切"故事。

FCV should remain the disease module where vaccine/immunity complexity, VS-FCV distinction, carrier persistence, and therapy limitations all stay visible at the top layer instead of collapsing into a simple vaccination-solves-everything story.
