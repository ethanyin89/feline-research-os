# 什么是猫杯状病毒感染？ / What is Feline Calicivirus Infection?

## 简单回答 / Simple Answer

**猫杯状病毒（FCV）是一种常见的猫科传染病，主要影响口腔和上呼吸道。**

FCV 是一种高度可变的 RNA 病毒，可以导致从无症状感染到严重系统性疾病的多种表现。这种病毒在猫群中非常普遍，是"猫流感"综合征的主要病因之一。

Feline calicivirus (FCV) is a common infectious disease in cats that primarily affects the mouth and upper respiratory tract.

FCV is a highly variable RNA virus that can cause presentations ranging from asymptomatic infection to severe systemic disease. This virus is very common in cat populations and is one of the main causes of "cat flu" syndrome.

[source_supported_conclusion: src-fcv-001, src-fcv-002]

## 有多常见？ / How Common Is It?

FCV 是全球最常见的猫科病毒病原体之一。

FCV is one of the most common viral pathogens of cats worldwide.

- 在多猫环境（如庇护所、繁殖场）中尤其普遍
- 部分猫即使健康也可能携带并排毒
- 各年龄段猫都可感染，但幼猫更易出现临床症状

- Especially common in multi-cat environments (shelters, breeding facilities)
- Some cats may carry and shed the virus even when healthy
- Cats of all ages can be infected, but kittens are more likely to show clinical signs

[source_supported_conclusion: src-fcv-001]

## 有什么症状？ / What Are the Signs?

FCV 的临床表现多样，从无症状到危及生命都有可能：

FCV clinical presentations vary widely, from asymptomatic to life-threatening:

### 典型症状 / Classic Signs

| 症状 | Sign |
|------|------|
| 口腔溃疡 | Oral ulceration |
| 流涕 | Nasal discharge |
| 打喷嚏 | Sneezing |
| 结膜炎 | Conjunctivitis |
| 发热 | Fever |
| 食欲下降 | Decreased appetite |

### 其他可能表现 / Other Possible Presentations

| 表现 | Presentation |
|------|--------------|
| 跛行（"跛行综合征"） | Limping ("limping syndrome") |
| 眼部疾病 | Ocular disease |
| 无症状携带 | Asymptomatic carriage |

### 严重系统性感染 / Virulent Systemic Disease

少数情况下，FCV 可引起严重系统性感染（VS-FCV），表现为：
- 高死亡率
- 多器官受累
- 皮肤溃疡和水肿

In rare cases, FCV can cause virulent systemic disease (VS-FCV), presenting with:
- High mortality
- Multi-organ involvement
- Skin ulceration and edema

[source_supported_conclusion: src-fcv-002, src-fcv-004]

## 如何传播？ / How Does It Spread?

FCV 主要通过以下途径传播：

FCV spreads mainly through:

| 途径 | Route |
|------|-------|
| 直接接触 | Direct contact with infected cats |
| 飞沫传播 | Respiratory droplets |
| 污染物品 | Contaminated fomites (bowls, bedding, hands) |
| 携带者猫 | Carrier cats shedding the virus |

**重要：** 部分康复猫可成为长期携带者，持续排毒数月甚至更长时间。

**Important:** Some recovered cats can become long-term carriers, shedding virus for months or even longer.

[source_supported_conclusion: src-fcv-002]

## 如何诊断？ / How Is It Diagnosed?

**诊断 FCV 需要综合考虑临床症状和实验室检测。**

**Diagnosing FCV requires considering both clinical signs and laboratory testing.**

| 检查 | 意义 |
|------|------|
| 临床检查 | 口腔溃疡 + 上呼吸道症状提示可能 |
| RT-PCR 检测 | 最佳检测工具，但解读需谨慎 |
| 病毒分离 | 确诊方法，但不常规使用 |

| Test | Significance |
|------|--------------|
| Clinical exam | Oral ulcers + upper respiratory signs suggest possible FCV |
| RT-PCR testing | Best detection tool, but interpretation requires caution |
| Virus isolation | Confirmatory but not routinely used |

### 诊断警示 / Diagnostic Caution

- 健康猫也可能检测阳性（携带者）
- 阴性结果不能完全排除感染
- 症状相似的其他病原体也需考虑

- Healthy cats can test positive (carriers)
- Negative results don't completely rule out infection
- Other pathogens with similar signs must be considered

[source_supported_conclusion: src-fcv-009]

## 可以治疗吗？ / Can It Be Treated?

**FCV 感染主要依靠支持性护理。目前没有针对 FCV 的特效抗病毒药物。**

**FCV infection relies mainly on supportive care. There is currently no specific antiviral medication for FCV.**

### 管理策略 / Management Strategies

| 策略 | 说明 |
|------|------|
| 支持性护理 | 液体、营养支持、保持鼻腔通畅 |
| 抗生素 | 治疗继发细菌感染，非治疗病毒本身 |
| 良好护理 | 保暖、喂食软食、清洁眼鼻分泌物 |
| 隔离 | 防止传染其他猫 |

| Strategy | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| Supportive care | Fluids, nutritional support, keeping nasal passages clear |
| Antibiotics | Treat secondary bacterial infections, not the virus itself |
| Good nursing care | Keep warm, offer soft food, clean ocular/nasal discharge |
| Isolation | Prevent spread to other cats |

**预后：** 大多数健康成年猫可康复。幼猫、老年猫或免疫功能低下的猫预后可能较差。

**Prognosis:** Most healthy adult cats recover. Kittens, elderly cats, or immunocompromised cats may have poorer outcomes.

[source_supported_conclusion: src-fcv-004]

## 可以预防吗？ / Can It Be Prevented?

**疫苗接种是预防 FCV 的重要工具，但有其局限性。**

**Vaccination is an important tool for preventing FCV, but it has limitations.**

### 疫苗作用 / What Vaccines Do

| 能做到 | 做不到 |
|--------|--------|
| 减少临床疾病严重程度 | 完全预防感染 |
| 降低死亡风险 | 防止所有毒株 |
| 减轻症状 | 阻止猫成为携带者 |

| Can do | Cannot do |
|--------|-----------|
| Reduce clinical disease severity | Completely prevent infection |
| Lower mortality risk | Protect against all strains |
| Lessen symptoms | Stop cats from becoming carriers |

### 其他预防措施 / Other Prevention Measures

| 措施 | Measure |
|------|---------|
| 环境卫生 | Environmental hygiene |
| 减少应激 | Stress reduction |
| 隔离新猫 | Quarantine new cats |
| 减少猫群密度 | Reduce population density |

[source_supported_conclusion: src-fcv-002, src-fcv-004]

## 下一步 / Next Step

**如果你担心猫可能感染FCV：**

1. 观察口腔、眼睛和呼吸道症状
2. 安排兽医检查
3. 保持环境清洁，隔离患病猫

**If you're concerned your cat may have FCV infection:**

1. Watch for oral, eye, and respiratory symptoms
2. Schedule a veterinary examination
3. Keep environment clean, isolate affected cats

**请注意：** 大多数FCV感染是自限性的。但VS-FCV（毒力系统性FCV）是紧急情况，需要立即就医。

**Note:** Most FCV infections are self-limiting. However, VS-FCV (virulent systemic FCV) is an emergency requiring immediate veterinary care.
