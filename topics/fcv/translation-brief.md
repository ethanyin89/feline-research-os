## Core Takeaway

FCV translation currently means supportive care plus bounded vaccine/control logic, while therapeutic claims remain exploratory and should stay below routine clinical recommendation.

## Current Safe Read

- vaccination remains a disease-reduction tool, not an infection-elimination tool
- supportive care and hygiene stay central
- interferon and antiviral/immunostimulatory papers are frontier branches, even though the branch now has assay-stage, interferon-sensitivity, and first in vivo anchors
- treatment language should remain below final ranking until full-text, field-effectiveness, and product/label evidence justify stronger implementation claims

## Working Translation Frame

The safest current FCV translation model is a layered one:

- prevention and exposure control
- symptom-support and nursing care
- vaccine-breadth and vaccine-break interpretation
- carrier/persistence boundary handling
- exploratory antiviral or immunomodulatory branches

That structure is stronger than any single drug or product ladder at the current FCV evidence depth.

## Translation Boundaries

- `src-fcv-004`, `src-fcv-007`, and `src-fcv-009` support FCV as a common, variable, and control-relevant feline pathogen with vaccination and hygiene as core management tools.
- `src-fcv-003`, `src-fcv-010`, `src-fcv-011`, `src-fcv-022`, and `src-fcv-024` support a real vaccine cross-neutralisation and heterologous-protection branch, but also block naive claims that one vaccine fully covers current field diversity.
- `src-fcv-017` supports persistence and immune-evasion logic, which is exactly why infection-block and long-term carriage claims should be treated more cautiously than disease-mitigation claims.
- `src-fcv-022` supports a cellular-immunity branch that explains why vaccine benefit can exceed simple neutralising-antibody breadth.
- `src-fcv-014` now provides the assay-stage discovery anchor, `src-fcv-008` provides the interferon-sensitivity caution anchor, and `src-fcv-018` provides the first deep-extracted in vivo therapy anchor.
- `src-fcv-007` now provides the main modern broad-control anchor that keeps vaccination inside an integrated strategy shaped by carrier-state and reinfection pressure.
- `src-fcv-009` now provides the diagnosis/control bridge that stabilizes `PCR-best-but-bounded` and `vaccination-core-but-non-sterilizing` wording.

## Guardrail

Do not collapse FCV control, vaccine, persistence, and exploratory therapy literature into one treatment ladder. The correct next gain is output-specific compression that separates disease reduction, shedding, reinfection/persistence, strain-coverage boundaries, and exploratory therapy evidence.

## Best Next Work

- keep vaccine and treatment language below current-market product ranking
- separate routine support, chronic oral disease, and severe-systemic rescue contexts before writing owner-facing guidance
