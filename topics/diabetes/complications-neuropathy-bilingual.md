## 本页回答的问题 / Question This Page Answers

如何纳入糖尿病神经病变而不将其变成治疗声明？

How should diabetic neuropathy be included without turning it into a treatment claim?

## 核心要点 / Core Takeaway

猫糖尿病神经病变应表示为并发症机制和终点分支，而非治疗声明或简单的跖行姿势标签。

Feline diabetic neuropathy should be represented as a complication mechanism and endpoint branch, not as a treatment claim or a simple plantigrade-posture label.

## 允许的用途 / Allowed Uses

神经病变可以：

- 锚定慢性并发症语言 / anchor chronic complication language
- 添加运动传导和组织病理终点 / add motor conduction and tissue-pathology endpoints
- 防止糖尿病页面过度关注缓解和血糖控制 / keep diabetes pages from over-focusing on remission and glycemic control alone
- 将临床体征与电生理、髓鞘、施万细胞、轴突和微血管发现联系起来 / connect clinical signs to electrophysiologic, myelin, Schwann-cell, axonal, and microvascular findings

## 尚不允许 / Not Allowed Yet

神经病变不应：

- 在没有更充分证据的情况下成为常规筛查建议 / become a routine screening recommendation without fuller evidence
- 暗示特定治疗或可逆性声明 / imply a specific treatment or reversibility claim
- 仅被视为跖行姿势 / be treated as only plantigrade posture
- 用于按并发症预防排名糖尿病治疗 / be used to rank diabetes treatments by complication prevention

## 声明标签 / Claim Labels

当提到神经病变时，将声明标记为：

- `临床体征 clinical sign`: 跖行姿势或步态异常
- `神经生理学 neurophysiology`: 传导或感觉运动功能障碍
- `组织病理学 tissue pathology`: 髓鞘、施万细胞、轴突或微血管变化
- `未知 unknown`: 可逆性、筛查或治疗效果尚未确立

## 护栏 / Guardrail

神经病变是并发症终点分支，不是治疗声明。勿暗示特定治疗或可逆性声明。可逆性、筛查或治疗效果尚未确立。

Neuropathy is a complication endpoint branch, not a treatment claim. Do not imply a specific treatment or reversibility claim. Reversibility, screening, or treatment effect is not yet established.
