# 猫糖尿病机制概述 / Feline Diabetes Mechanism Overview

## 工作框架 / Working Frame

猫糖尿病应作为胰岛素抵抗加 β 细胞功能障碍模块开始。胰岛素敏感性降低增加分泌需求，但显性糖尿病仍取决于胰岛素分泌不足，肥胖、内分泌疾病、胰腺炎和物种特异性胰岛病理作为分支压力。

Feline diabetes should start as an insulin-resistance plus beta-cell dysfunction module. Reduced insulin sensitivity increases secretory demand, but overt diabetes depends on inadequate insulin secretion, with obesity, endocrine disease, pancreatitis, and species-specific islet pathology acting as branch pressures.

## 首次机制主线 / First-Pass Mechanism Spine

```
肥胖/内分泌疾病/胰腺炎
    ↓
胰岛素敏感性降低或需求改变
    ↓
β 细胞应激/衰竭
    ↓
显性糖尿病
```

**关键来源支持 Key source support:**

- `src-diabetes-002`, `src-diabetes-019`: 胰岛淀粉样变、β 细胞变性、猫特异性发病机制
- `src-diabetes-013`, `src-diabetes-020`: 防止过度压缩所有糖尿病猫为默认 2 型样框架

## 物种特异性考虑 / Species-Specific Considerations

- 猫胰岛淀粉样变沉积 / Feline islet amyloid deposition
- β 细胞变性模式 / Beta-cell degeneration patterns
- 2 型样表现但具有独特发病机制 / Type-2-like presentation with unique pathogenesis

## 护栏 / Guardrail

不要让缓解潜力抹去慢性疾病生物学。机制页应解释为何一些猫可能缓解，同时仍保留 β 细胞衰竭、肥胖压力、胰腺炎和内分泌继发性糖尿病。

Do not let remission potential erase chronic disease biology. The mechanism page should explain why some cats may remit while still preserving beta-cell failure, obesity pressure, pancreatitis, and endocrine-secondary diabetes.
