## 工作框架 / Working Frame

早期识别不应仅归结为高血糖。第一批来源集指向涉及肥胖、品种/种群风险、胰腺炎、高生长激素血症/肢端肥大症和更广泛胰腺疾病背景的风险图谱。

Early recognition should not be reduced to hyperglycemia alone. The first-pass source set points toward a risk map involving obesity, breed/population risk, pancreatitis, hypersomatotropism/acromegaly, and broader pancreatic disease context.

## 继发性疾病门控 / Secondary-Disease Gate

大多数糖尿病猫可能符合 2 型样框架，但有些有其他特定糖尿病病因。高生长激素血症诱导的糖尿病是由生长激素相关胰岛素抵抗驱动的主要继发性糖尿病问题。识别应因此为高生长激素血症/肢端肥大症和高肾上腺皮质功能亢进保留分支，特别是当临床过程、胰岛素反应或缓解预期不符合默认框架时。

Most diabetic cats may fit a type-2-like frame, but some have other specific diabetes etiologies. Hypersomatotropism-induced diabetes is a major secondary diabetes problem driven by growth-hormone-related insulin resistance. Recognition should keep a branch for hypersomatotropism/acromegaly and hyperadrenocorticism, especially when clinical course, insulin response, or remission expectations do not fit the default frame.

## 肥胖门控 / Obesity Gate

肥胖是机制关联的风险分支，不是一般的生活方式注释。体况属于风险识别，因为肥胖可降低胰岛素敏感性并与 β 细胞功能障碍相互作用，而当前表现可能包括体重或肌肉流失。

Obesity is a mechanism-linked risk branch, not a generic lifestyle note. Body condition belongs in risk recognition because obesity can reduce insulin sensitivity and interact with beta-cell dysfunction, while current presentation may include weight or muscle loss.

## 胰腺炎门控 / Pancreatitis Gate

胰腺炎是双向共病分支。与糖尿病的关联频繁，但确切因果关系未确定。识别页面应因此将胰腺炎作为管理复杂化共病保留，而不声称单一因果方向。

Pancreatitis is a bidirectional comorbidity branch. The association with diabetes is frequent, but exact causality is not settled. Recognition pages should preserve pancreatitis as a management-complicating comorbidity without claiming a single causal direction.

## 品种风险信号 / Breed-Risk Signal

品种风险综合备忘录结合了来自 src-diabetes-009 的英国受保人群缅甸猫信号和来自 src-diabetes-012 的澳大利亚诊所期缅甸猫信号。这足够用于品种风险综合分支，但不足以声称机制或普遍人群患病率。

The breed-risk synthesis memo combines the UK insured-population Burmese signal and the Australian clinic-period Burmese signal. This is strong enough for a breed-risk synthesis branch, but not enough to claim a mechanism or universal population prevalence.

## 检查路由门控 / Workup Routing Gate

诊断监测检查备忘录现在拥有识别到检查的架构。它通过诊断确认加体况状态、继发性内分泌疾病、胰腺炎/DKA 复杂性、治疗候选性和监测强度路由糖尿病问题。

The diagnostic monitoring workup memo now owns the recognition-to-workup architecture. It routes diabetes questions through diagnosis confirmation plus body-condition state, secondary endocrine disease, pancreatitis/DKA complexity, treatment candidacy, and monitoring intensity.

## 护栏 / Guardrail

患病率和品种风险声明在流行病学论文深度阅读前保持暂定。本页不应在没有证据的情况下将人群研究转化为个体猫预测规则。

Prevalence and breed-risk claims stay provisional until the epidemiology papers are deep-read. This page should not turn population studies into individual-cat prediction rules without evidence.
