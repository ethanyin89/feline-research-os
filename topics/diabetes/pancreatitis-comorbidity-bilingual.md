# 猫糖尿病与胰腺炎共病 / Feline Diabetes And Pancreatitis Comorbidity

## 本页回答的问题 / Question This Page Answers

胰腺炎在猫糖尿病模块中应如何表示？

How should pancreatitis be represented in the feline diabetes module?

## 核心要点 / Core Takeaway

糖尿病和胰腺炎应建模为可使糖尿病管理和 DKA 护理复杂化的双向共病问题，而非单向因果解释。

Diabetes and pancreatitis should be modeled as a bidirectional comorbidity problem that can complicate diabetes management and DKA care, not as a one-way causal explanation.

## 证据拥有者 / Evidence Owners

- src-diabetes-010: 主要猫糖尿病-胰腺炎共病锚点
- src-diabetes-023: 跨物种胰腺疾病和酮症酸中毒背景

## 当前分支含义 / Current Branch Meaning

胰腺炎应出现在：

- 风险和识别，因为它可能与糖尿病共存并使表现复杂化 / risk and recognition, because it may coexist with diabetes and complicate presentation
- 转化，因为当胰腺炎共存时糖尿病管理可能变得更难 / translation, because diabetes management can become harder when pancreatitis coexists
- 综合，因为它阻止了一条清晰的单一途径糖尿病叙事 / synthesis, because it blocks a clean one-pathway diabetes story
- DKA 讨论，因为胰腺炎可能增加管理复杂性 / DKA discussion, because pancreatitis may increase management complexity

## 模块现在可以说什么 / What The Module Can Say Now

- 胰腺炎是高优先级共病分支 / pancreatitis is a high-priority comorbidity branch
- 因果关系应保持谨慎 / causality should remain cautious
- 双向关联是当前最安全的框架 / bidirectional association is the safest current frame
- 并发胰腺炎可使糖尿病和 DKA 管理更困难 / concurrent pancreatitis can make diabetes and DKA management harder

## 模块尚不应说什么 / What The Module Should Not Say Yet

- 勿声称糖尿病导致胰腺炎或胰腺炎导致糖尿病作为确定的单向途径
- 勿仅从当前编译层提供胰腺炎诊断算法
- 勿在全文审查前量化结果影响
- 勿将胰腺炎与内分泌继发性糖尿病合并

## 护栏 / Guardrail

胰腺炎是双向共病分支，不是单向因果解释。勿声称确定的因果方向。并发胰腺炎可使糖尿病和 DKA 管理更困难。

Pancreatitis is a bidirectional comorbidity branch, not a one-way causal explanation. Do not claim a settled causal direction. Concurrent pancreatitis can make diabetes and DKA management harder.
