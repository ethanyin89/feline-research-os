# 什么是猫糖尿病？ / What is Feline Diabetes?

## Quantified Claim Traceability

| Claim | Source IDs | Boundary |
|---|---|---|
| Fructosamine reflects average blood sugar over 1-2 weeks | src-diabetes-001 | Diagnostic marker timeframe; individual variation exists |

## 简单回答 / Simple Answer

**猫糖尿病是一种代谢疾病，猫的身体无法正常利用血糖。**

正常情况下，胰岛素帮助血糖进入细胞供能。糖尿病猫要么产生胰岛素不足，要么身体对胰岛素不敏感（胰岛素抵抗），导致血糖升高。

Feline diabetes mellitus is a metabolic disease where a cat's body cannot properly use blood sugar.

Normally, insulin helps blood sugar enter cells for energy. Diabetic cats either don't produce enough insulin, or their body doesn't respond to insulin properly (insulin resistance), causing high blood sugar.

## 有多常见？ / How Common Is It?

猫糖尿病是常见的内分泌疾病，近年来发病率有上升趋势。

Feline diabetes is a common endocrine disease, with increasing prevalence in recent years.

- 肥胖猫风险显著增加
- 中老年猫更常见
- 公猫可能风险略高
- 某些品种可能更易患病（如缅甸猫）

- Obese cats have significantly increased risk
- More common in middle-aged to older cats
- Male cats may have slightly higher risk
- Some breeds may be predisposed (e.g., Burmese)

[source_supported_conclusion: src-diabetes-001, src-diabetes-020]

## 有什么症状？ / What Are the Signs?

糖尿病的典型症状被称为"四多"：

The classic symptoms of diabetes are known as the "four polys":

### 典型症状 / Classic Signs

| 症状 | Sign |
|------|------|
| 多饮 | Increased thirst (polydipsia) |
| 多尿 | Increased urination (polyuria) |
| 多食 | Increased appetite (polyphagia) |
| 体重下降 | Weight loss despite eating more |

### 其他可能症状 / Other Possible Signs

| 症状 | Sign |
|------|------|
| 被毛变差 | Poor coat condition |
| 精神变差 | Lethargy |
| 后腿无力/站姿异常 | Weakness/abnormal stance in hind legs |
| 不愿跳跃 | Reluctance to jump |

**后腿问题说明：** 糖尿病可导致神经病变，表现为后腿无力、以脚跟着地的异常步态。

**About hind leg issues:** Diabetes can cause neuropathy, presenting as hind leg weakness and an abnormal plantigrade stance (walking on heels).

[source_supported_conclusion: src-diabetes-001]

## 为什么会发生？ / Why Does It Happen?

猫糖尿病通常是多因素的：

Feline diabetes is typically multifactorial:

### 主要风险因素 / Major Risk Factors

| 因素 | 说明 |
|------|------|
| 肥胖 | 最重要的风险因素，导致胰岛素抵抗 |
| 年龄 | 中老年猫更常见 |
| 缺乏运动 | 室内久坐生活方式 |
| 遗传 | 某些品种风险更高 |

| Factor | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Obesity | Most important risk factor, causes insulin resistance |
| Age | More common in middle-aged to older cats |
| Inactivity | Indoor sedentary lifestyle |
| Genetics | Some breeds at higher risk |

### 其他相关因素 / Other Contributing Factors

| 因素 | Factor |
|------|--------|
| 胰腺炎 | Pancreatitis |
| 肢端肥大症 | Acromegaly (excess growth hormone) |
| 甲亢 | Hyperthyroidism |
| 长期使用类固醇 | Long-term steroid use |

[source_supported_conclusion: src-diabetes-005, src-diabetes-010]

## 如何诊断？ / How Is It Diagnosed?

诊断需要结合症状和检查结果：

Diagnosis requires combining symptoms with test results:

| 检查 | 意义 |
|------|------|
| 血糖测量 | 持续升高提示糖尿病 |
| 果糖胺 | 反映过去 1-2 周平均血糖 |
| 尿液检查 | 可见尿糖、可能有尿酮 |
| 全面血液检查 | 排除其他疾病 |

| Test | Significance |
|------|--------------|
| Blood glucose | Persistent elevation suggests diabetes |
| Fructosamine | Reflects average blood sugar over 1-2 weeks |
| Urinalysis | May show glucose, possibly ketones |
| Complete blood work | Rule out other conditions |

**注意：** 猫可能因应激出现暂时性高血糖，因此诊断需要综合判断。

**Note:** Cats can have stress-induced temporary hyperglycemia, so diagnosis requires comprehensive evaluation.

[source_supported_conclusion: src-diabetes-001]

## 可以治疗吗？ / Can It Be Treated?

**是的，猫糖尿病可以治疗，部分猫甚至可以"缓解"（不再需要胰岛素）。**

**Yes, feline diabetes can be treated, and some cats can even achieve "remission" (no longer need insulin).**

### 主要治疗方法 / Main Treatments

| 方法 | 说明 |
|------|------|
| 胰岛素注射 | 最常见的治疗，需要主人学会注射 |
| 饮食调整 | 低碳水化合物饮食很重要 |
| 减肥 | 如果超重，减肥可改善胰岛素敏感性 |
| 口服药物 | 某些情况可用（如 SGLT2 抑制剂） |
| 定期监测 | 血糖曲线和定期复查 |

| Method | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Insulin injections | Most common treatment, owners learn to inject |
| Diet modification | Low-carbohydrate diet is important |
| Weight loss | If overweight, losing weight improves insulin sensitivity |
| Oral medications | Available in some cases (e.g., SGLT2 inhibitors) |
| Regular monitoring | Blood glucose curves and regular checkups |

### 关于缓解 / About Remission

- 部分猫经过早期积极治疗后可停用胰岛素
- 并非所有猫都能缓解
- 缓解与早期诊断、严格饮食控制、良好血糖管理相关
- 曾经缓解的猫可能复发

- Some cats can stop insulin after early aggressive treatment
- Not all cats achieve remission
- Remission is associated with early diagnosis, strict diet control, and good glucose management
- Cats that achieved remission may relapse

[source_supported_conclusion: src-diabetes-001, src-diabetes-020]

## 下一步 / Next Step

**如果你担心猫可能有糖尿病：**

1. 记录饮水量、排尿量、食欲和体重变化
2. 安排兽医检查，包括血糖和果糖胺检测
3. 不要自行减少猫的食物摄入

**If you're concerned your cat may have diabetes:**

1. Document water intake, urination, appetite, and weight changes
2. Schedule a vet exam including blood glucose and fructosamine tests
3. Do not reduce your cat's food intake on your own

**请注意：** 早期诊断和积极治疗可以提高缓解机会。尽早就医很重要。

**Note:** Early diagnosis and aggressive treatment can improve chances of remission. Seeking care early is important.
