## 当前综合 / Current Synthesis

猫糖尿病最好作为具有慢性代谢和内分泌约束的可逆潜力疾病开始。已完成 24/24 轮次 1 提取强化综合：缓解是真实的，饮食和胰岛素选择很重要，但 β 细胞衰竭、肥胖驱动的胰岛素抵抗、胰腺炎、内分泌继发性糖尿病、神经病变和 SGLT2 安全边界使模块不能成为单一缓解方案故事。

Feline diabetes is best started as a reversible-potential disease. Remission is real, diet and insulin choices matter, but beta-cell failure, obesity-driven insulin resistance, pancreatitis, endocrine-secondary diabetes, neuropathy, and SGLT2 safety boundaries keep the module from becoming a single remission protocol story.
