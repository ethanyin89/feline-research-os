## Strongest Current Conclusions / 当前最稳的结论

### Core Backbone / 核心骨架

**EN**
- FIP is best modeled as a disease where `diagnostic ambiguity` and `treatment transformation` must stay visible at the same time.
- The first major compression problem is not one mutation, one test, or one drug, but how to separate `pathobiogenesis`, `supportive recognition`, `bounded mutation utility`, and `antiviral actionability`.
- The strongest current top-layer architecture is:
  - pathobiogenesis and macrophage-linked systemic disease competence
  - clinicopathology-led suspicion and disease-form recognition
  - mutation support bounded by diagnostic architecture
  - antiviral treatment reality that does not erase workup uncertainty
- the endpoint branch is now clearer as `support order + assay boundary`, not as a final assay leaderboard

**ZH**
- FIP 最适合被建模成一个必须同时保留 `diagnostic ambiguity` 和 `treatment transformation` 的 disease module。
- 当前第一位的压缩问题不是某一个 mutation、某一个测试或某一种药，而是如何分开 `pathobiogenesis`、`supportive recognition`、`bounded mutation utility` 和 `antiviral actionability`。
- 当前最稳的顶层架构是：
  - pathobiogenesis 与 macrophage-linked systemic disease competence
  - 由 clinicopathology 主导的怀疑与 disease-form 识别
  - 被诊断架构约束住的 mutation support
  - 不会抹去 workup 不确定性的抗病毒治疗现实
- endpoint 分支现在更清楚地被压成 `support order + assay boundary`，而不是一个最终 assay leaderboard

### Mechanism And Diagnosis / 机制与诊断

**EN**
- mutation-origin belongs to mechanism first, not to diagnostic certainty first
- recombinant and alternative emergence logic matter because they keep the mechanism branch from collapsing into one simple route
- diagnosis still belongs to a composite, supportive architecture rather than a one-test claim

**ZH**
- mutation-origin 首先属于 mechanism，而不是首先属于 diagnostic certainty
- recombinant 和其他 emergence logic 很重要，因为它们阻止机制分支塌缩成单一路径故事
- diagnosis 仍然属于一个复合的 supportive architecture，而不是 one-test claim

### Recognition And Disease Form / 识别与病型

**EN**
- clinicopathology remains one of the strongest operational branches
- wet, dry, and neurologic FIP should stay visible as workup-shaping branches, not as one flat severity story
- CSF and mutation-associated tests can support narrowed contexts without becoming general front doors

**ZH**
- clinicopathology 仍然是最强的 operational branch 之一
- 湿性、干性和神经型 FIP 应继续作为改变 workup 方向的分支存在，而不是一个平面的严重度故事
- CSF 和 mutation 相关测试可以在缩窄后的情境里提供支持，但不应变成 general front door

### Treatment / 治疗层

**EN**
- the antiviral treatment branch is real and unusually strong
- the safest current frame is `treatment transformation without diagnostic simplification`
- experimental foundation, baseline GS efficacy, real-world packages, neurologic rescue, and remission durability should stay separated

**ZH**
- 抗病毒治疗分支是真实存在而且异常强的
- 当前最稳的框架是 `treatment transformation without diagnostic simplification`
- experimental foundation、baseline GS efficacy、real-world package、neurologic rescue 和 remission durability 应继续保持分层

### Risk And Context / 风险与背景

**EN**
- signalment and ecology-aware risk can raise suspicion earlier
- referral-population enrichment matters, but should not be confused with baseline prevalence
- risk context should accelerate workup, not settle diagnosis

**ZH**
- signalment 和 ecology-aware risk 可以更早提高怀疑度
- referral-population enrichment 很重要，但不应和 baseline prevalence 混为一谈
- risk context 应该加速 workup，而不是替代 diagnosis

## What We Can Defend / 当前可以稳妥主张的内容

**EN**
- modern treatment transformation does not remove diagnostic ambiguity
- mutation utility should stay bounded by the wider diagnostic architecture
- disease-form branching is operationally important
- clinicopathology should lead suspicion-pattern recognition before bounded molecular strengthening
- treatment optimism and regulatory or route certainty must stay separate

**ZH**
- 现代治疗转变并不会消除诊断不确定性
- mutation utility 应保持在更宽的诊断架构约束之下
- disease-form branching 在 operational 上很重要
- clinicopathology 应先主导怀疑模式识别，再进入有边界的分子增强
- treatment optimism 与 regulatory 或 route certainty 必须保持分开
