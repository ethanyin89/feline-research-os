## Current Recognition Architecture / 当前识别架构

### Entry Frame / 进入框架

**EN**
- recognition should begin from supportive clinical suspicion, not from one mutation or one marker
- FIP suspicion is not the same thing as diagnostic closure
- risk context and recognition logic should stay adjacent but separate

**ZH**
- 识别起点应是 supportive clinical suspicion，而不是某一个 mutation 或 marker
- FIP suspicion 不等于 diagnostic closure
- risk context 和 recognition logic 应继续相邻但分开

### Risk-Context Layer / 风险背景层

**EN**
- age, breed pattern, sex pattern, and ecology-aware exposure belong here
- multi-cat environment and endemic coronavirus burden can raise suspicion earlier
- referral-population enrichment belongs to context, not to disease proof

**ZH**
- 年龄、品种模式、性别模式和 ecology-aware exposure 属于这一层
- 多猫环境和地方性 coronavirus 负担可以更早提高怀疑度
- referral-population enrichment 属于 context，而不是 disease proof

### Clinicopathology-Led Recognition Layer / 由临床病理主导的识别层

**EN**
- clinicopathology remains the strongest operational branch
- staged pattern recognition should lead before bounded molecular strengthening
- effusive and non-effusive patterning belongs inside recognition, not after diagnosis is already settled

**ZH**
- clinicopathology 仍然是最强的 operational branch
- staged pattern recognition 应先主导，再进入有边界的 molecular strengthening
- effusive 和 non-effusive 的模式分化应放在 recognition 内部，而不是等 diagnosis 已经坐实之后

### Disease-Form Branching / 病型分支

**EN**
- wet, dry, and neurologic FIP should stay visible as workup-shaping branches
- neurologic disease is not just more severe FIP, but a branch with different support logic
- CSF support belongs after the workup has already shifted into a neurologic context
- the real boundary is now clearer: ordinary workup should end before CSF logic becomes central

**ZH**
- 湿性、干性和神经型 FIP 应继续作为改变 workup 方向的分支存在
- 神经型疾病不只是更重的 FIP，而是一个具有不同支持逻辑的分支
- CSF 支持应放在 workup 已经转入神经系统语境之后
- 现在更清楚的边界是：ordinary workup 应先结束，CSF 逻辑才会变成中心

### Boundary-Aware Layer / 边界意识层

**EN**
- mutation support can strengthen suspicion without becoming certainty
- recognition should stay explicitly below one-test language
- diagnosis should remain composite even in the treatment era

**ZH**
- mutation support 可以增强怀疑，但不能变成 certainty
- recognition 应继续明确放在 one-test language 之下
- 即使进入治疗时代，diagnosis 也应继续保持 composite

## What We Can Defend / 当前可以稳妥主张的内容

**EN**
- FIP recognition is more about supportive architecture than about single-assay compression
- risk context should accelerate suspicion without settling diagnosis
- clinicopathology should lead practical recognition
- disease-form branching is operationally important

**ZH**
- FIP 的识别更像 supportive architecture，而不是 single-assay compression
- risk context 应加速怀疑，但不能替代 diagnosis
- clinicopathology 应继续主导 practical recognition
- disease-form branching 在 operational 上很重要

## What We Should Not Overstate / 当前不应说过头的内容

**EN**
- no current recognition layer should be rewritten as one-test diagnosis
- mutation-positive does not equal definitive FIP
- referral enrichment is not population-level certainty
- CSF support is not general workup leadership

**ZH**
- 当前没有任何识别层应被改写成 one-test diagnosis
- mutation-positive 不等于 definitive FIP
- referral enrichment 不等于 population-level certainty
- CSF 支持不是 general workup leadership
