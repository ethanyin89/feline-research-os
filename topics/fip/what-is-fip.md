# 什么是FIP（猫传染性腹膜炎）？ / What is FIP (Feline Infectious Peritonitis)?

## Quantified Claim Traceability

| Claim | Source IDs | Boundary |
|---|---|---|
| GS-441524 treatment duration is 84 days (12 weeks) | src-fip-003 | Standard protocol duration; individual cases may vary based on clinical response |
| Molnupiravir equivalent to GS-441524 (no significant efficacy difference, p=0.326) | src-fip-028 | 118-cat comparative cohort; both drugs achieve ~99% remission in completers |

## 简单回答 / Simple Answer

**FIP是一种由冠状病毒变异引起的猫的致命疾病。** 大多数猫感染冠状病毒后只会轻微腹泻或没有症状，但在少数猫体内，病毒会发生变异，引发全身性的严重炎症反应，这就是FIP。

**FIP is a fatal disease in cats caused by a mutated coronavirus.** Most cats infected with coronavirus only have mild diarrhea or no symptoms, but in a small number of cats, the virus mutates and triggers a severe systemic inflammatory response — this is FIP.

## 哪些猫容易得FIP？ / Which Cats Are at Risk?

| 高风险因素 | Risk Factor | 说明 / Description |
|-----------|-------------|-------------------|
| 年龄 | Age | 1岁以下的幼猫风险最高 / Kittens under 1 year at highest risk |
| 环境 | Environment | 多猫家庭、猫舍、收容所 / Multi-cat homes, catteries, shelters |
| 压力 | Stress | 刚到新家、手术后、其他疾病 / New home, post-surgery, other illness |
| 品种 | Breed | 某些纯种猫风险略高 / Some purebreds at slightly higher risk |

**重要提示：** 有这些风险因素不代表一定会得FIP，只是需要多留意。

**Important:** Having these risk factors doesn't mean a cat will definitely get FIP — just that extra vigilance is warranted.

## FIP有什么症状？ / What Are the Signs?

FIP的症状分为两种类型：

FIP symptoms fall into two types:

### "湿性"FIP（腹水型） / "Wet" FIP (Effusive)

| 症状 | Sign | 表现 / Presentation |
|------|------|---------------------|
| 肚子变大 | Abdominal distension | 腹部积液，像怀孕一样鼓起来 / Fluid buildup, belly swells like pregnancy |
| 呼吸困难 | Difficulty breathing | 胸腔积液导致 / Caused by chest fluid |
| 精神差 | Lethargy | 不爱动，嗜睡 / Inactive, sleepy |
| 食欲下降 | Loss of appetite | 不想吃东西 / Refusing food |
| 发烧 | Fever | 反复发烧，药物难以控制 / Recurring fever, hard to control with medication |

### "干性"FIP（非腹水型） / "Dry" FIP (Non-effusive)

| 症状 | Sign | 表现 / Presentation |
|------|------|---------------------|
| 眼睛问题 | Eye problems | 眼睛浑浊、瞳孔颜色改变、怕光 / Cloudy eyes, color change, light sensitivity |
| 神经症状 | Neurological signs | 走路不稳、抽搐、行为异常 / Unsteady gait, seizures, behavior changes |
| 器官肿块 | Organ masses | 肝、肾、肠道等出现肉芽肿 / Granulomas in liver, kidneys, intestines |
| 持续发烧 | Persistent fever | 长期低烧或反复发烧 / Chronic low-grade or recurring fever |
| 消瘦 | Weight loss | 虽然在吃但越来越瘦 / Losing weight despite eating |

**注意：** 这些症状也可能是其他疾病。出现这些症状请尽快就医。

**Note:** These symptoms can also be caused by other diseases. Seek veterinary care promptly if you notice these signs.

## FIP能治吗？ / Can FIP Be Treated?

**是的，现在有药物可以治疗FIP。**

**Yes, there are now medications that can treat FIP.**

- **GS-441524** 和 **Molnupiravir（莫诺拉韦）** 都是有效的抗病毒药物
- 研究显示两种药物疗效相当（118只猫的对比研究，p=0.326）
- 治疗需要持续84天（12周）
- 早期发现、早期治疗效果更好
- 神经型/眼部FIP可能需要更高剂量

- **GS-441524** and **Molnupiravir** are both effective antiviral drugs
- Studies show both drugs have equivalent efficacy (118-cat comparative study, p=0.326)
- Treatment requires 84 days (12 weeks)
- Earlier detection and treatment leads to better outcomes
- Neurological/ocular FIP may require higher doses

**重要提示：** 治疗需要在兽医指导下进行。具体药物选择和剂量须由兽医根据病情决定。

**Important:** Treatment must be conducted under veterinary supervision. Specific drug choice and dosing must be determined by a veterinarian based on the individual case.

## 我该怎么做？ / What Should I Do?

### 如果怀疑FIP / If You Suspect FIP

1. **尽快就医** — 带猫去看兽医 / **See a vet promptly** — Take your cat to a veterinarian
2. **详细描述** — 告诉医生所有症状和时间线 / **Describe in detail** — Tell the vet all symptoms and timeline
3. **做检查** — 医生可能需要抽血、超声、甚至抽腹水 / **Get tests** — Blood work, ultrasound, possibly fluid analysis
4. **不要放弃** — FIP现在可以治疗 / **Don't give up** — FIP is now treatable

### 预防FIP / Preventing FIP

| 措施 | Measure |
|------|---------|
| 减少猫咪的压力 | Reduce stress |
| 保持环境清洁 | Keep environment clean |
| 新猫隔离观察 | Quarantine and observe new cats |
| 定期体检 | Regular health checkups |

## 下一步 / Next Step

**如果你担心猫可能有FIP：**

1. 立即预约兽医检查（FIP是紧急情况）
2. 记录所有症状（发烧、食欲、腹围变化、精神状态）
3. 不要延误 — 早期治疗效果更好

**If you're concerned your cat may have FIP:**

1. Schedule a vet appointment immediately (FIP is urgent)
2. Document all symptoms (fever, appetite, abdominal changes, behavior)
3. Don't delay — early treatment has better outcomes

**请注意：** FIP现在可以用GS-441524或Molnupiravir等抗病毒药治疗，两者疗效相当。不要放弃希望。

**Note:** FIP is now treatable with antiviral drugs (GS-441524 or Molnupiravir — both equally effective). Don't give up hope.
