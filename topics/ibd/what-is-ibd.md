# 什么是猫炎症性肠病？ / What is Feline IBD?

## 简单回答 / Simple Answer

**猫炎症性肠病（IBD）是一种慢性肠道炎症，导致猫出现长期消化道问题。**

这不是一种单一疾病，而是一组导致肠道壁慢性炎症的疾病。炎症会干扰正常的消化和营养吸收。

Feline inflammatory bowel disease (IBD) is chronic intestinal inflammation that causes long-term digestive problems in cats.

It's not a single disease, but a group of conditions causing chronic inflammation of the intestinal wall. This inflammation interferes with normal digestion and nutrient absorption.

[source_supported_conclusion: src-ibd-001]

## 有多常见？ / How Common Is It?

IBD 是猫最常见的慢性肠道疾病之一。

- 任何年龄都可能发生，但多见于中老年猫
- 各种品种都可能受影响
- 通常属于更广泛的"慢性肠病"范畴

IBD is one of the most common chronic intestinal diseases in cats.

- Can occur at any age, but more common in middle-aged to older cats
- All breeds can be affected
- Usually falls under the broader category of "chronic enteropathy"

[source_supported_conclusion: src-ibd-001]

## 有什么症状？ / What Are the Signs?

IBD 的症状通常是慢性的、反复出现的：

IBD symptoms are typically chronic and recurring:

### 常见症状 / Common Signs

| 症状 | Sign |
|------|------|
| 呕吐 | Vomiting |
| 腹泻 | Diarrhea |
| 体重下降 | Weight loss |
| 食欲变化 | Appetite changes |
| 精神变差 | Lethargy |

### 可能的表现 / Possible Presentations

| 表现 | Presentation |
|------|--------------|
| 大便带血或黏液 | Blood or mucus in stool |
| 腹部不适 | Abdominal discomfort |
| 毛发粗糙 | Poor coat condition |
| 排便频率改变 | Changes in bowel frequency |

**注意：** 这些症状并不特异，许多其他疾病也会导致类似问题。

**Note:** These symptoms are not specific to IBD; many other conditions cause similar problems.

[source_supported_conclusion: src-ibd-003]

## 如何诊断？ / How Is It Diagnosed?

**IBD 是一种排除性诊断。** 医生需要先排除其他可能的原因。

**IBD is a diagnosis of exclusion.** The veterinarian must first rule out other possible causes.

### 诊断步骤 / Diagnostic Steps

| 步骤 | 目的 |
|------|------|
| 病史和体检 | 了解症状模式 |
| 血液检查 | 排除代谢问题、评估整体健康 |
| 粪便检查 | 排除寄生虫感染 |
| 影像检查（超声/X光） | 评估肠道结构 |
| 饮食试验 | 排除食物过敏/不耐受 |
| 肠道活检 | 确认诊断的金标准 |

| Step | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| History and physical exam | Understand symptom patterns |
| Blood tests | Rule out metabolic issues, assess overall health |
| Fecal examination | Rule out parasites |
| Imaging (ultrasound/X-ray) | Assess intestinal structure |
| Diet trial | Rule out food allergy/intolerance |
| Intestinal biopsy | Gold standard for confirmation |

### 重要区分 / Important Distinction

IBD 需要与**肠道淋巴瘤**（一种肠道癌症）区分。两者症状相似，有时甚至组织病理学上也难以区分。

IBD must be distinguished from **intestinal lymphoma** (an intestinal cancer). Symptoms are similar, and sometimes even histopathology can be challenging to differentiate.

[source_supported_conclusion: src-ibd-010, src-ibd-015]

## 可以治疗吗？ / Can It Be Treated?

**IBD 通常可以管理，但无法完全治愈。** 目标是控制症状，提高生活质量。

**IBD can usually be managed, but not cured.** The goal is to control symptoms and improve quality of life.

### 管理策略 / Management Strategies

| 策略 | 说明 |
|------|------|
| 饮食调整 | 最常见的一线治疗 |
| 药物治疗 | 免疫抑制剂或抗炎药 |
| 益生菌 | 可能有辅助作用 |
| 维生素补充 | 如有吸收不良 |

| Strategy | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| Dietary modification | Most common first-line treatment |
| Medications | Immunosuppressants or anti-inflammatory drugs |
| Probiotics | May have supportive role |
| Vitamin supplementation | If malabsorption present |

**关键：** 饮食调整是最常见的起始治疗。很多猫对特殊饮食有反应，可能不需要长期用药。

**Key point:** Dietary modification is the most common starting treatment. Many cats respond to special diets and may not need long-term medication.

[source_supported_conclusion: src-ibd-001, src-ibd-003]

## 预后如何？ / What Is the Prognosis?

- 许多猫对治疗反应良好
- 可能需要终身管理
- 定期复查很重要
- 早期诊断和治疗效果更好

- Many cats respond well to treatment
- May require lifelong management
- Regular follow-up is important
- Earlier diagnosis and treatment often leads to better outcomes

[source_supported_conclusion: src-ibd-001]

## 下一步 / Next Step

**如果你担心猫可能有IBD：**

1. 记录所有消化道症状（呕吐、腹泻频率、食欲变化）
2. 安排兽医检查，可能需要做血检和超声
3. 不要自行改变饮食或停止喂食

**If you're concerned your cat may have IBD:**

1. Document all GI symptoms (vomiting, diarrhea frequency, appetite changes)
2. Schedule a vet exam; blood tests and ultrasound may be needed
3. Do not change diet or stop feeding on your own

**请注意：** IBD症状与其他疾病（包括肠道淋巴瘤）相似。确诊通常需要内镜活检。

**Note:** IBD symptoms overlap with other conditions (including intestinal lymphoma). Definitive diagnosis usually requires endoscopic biopsy.
