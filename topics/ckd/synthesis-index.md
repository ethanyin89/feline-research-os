## 1. 当前最强结论 / 1. Strongest Current Conclusions

### 核心文献骨架 / Core-Paper Backbone

**EN**
- The current strongest internal backbone comes from three papers used together:
  - `src-ckd-004` for operational clinical logic
  - `src-ckd-010` for clinicopathology correlation
  - `src-ckd-011` for fibrosis-centered mechanism framing
- The value of this trio is that they answer different layers of the same disease rather than repeating each other.

**ZH**
- 目前最强的内部学术骨架来自三篇文献的联合使用：
  - `src-ckd-004` 提供临床操作逻辑
  - `src-ckd-010` 提供临床病理相关性
  - `src-ckd-011` 提供以纤维化为中心的病理机制框架
- 这一组合的价值在于，它们分别解答了同一疾病的不同层面，而非简单的重复。

### 机制层 / Mechanism

**EN**
- `Renal fibrosis / tubulointerstitial fibrosis` is the strongest current mechanism backbone.
- The current vault supports treating fibrosis as the most stable anchor connecting structure, progression, and measurable disease burden.
- `TGF-beta` is now the strongest current mediator-level expansion target, but it still sits below fibrosis in certainty.

**ZH**
- “肾脏纤维化 / 肾小管间质纤维化”是目前最明确的病理机制主干。
- 当前知识库支持将纤维化作为连接结构病变、病程进展和可测量疾病负荷的最稳妥锚点。
- `TGF-beta` 是目前文献支持度最高的介质层扩展研究靶点，但在确定性上仍低于纤维化本身。

### 终点指标层 / Endpoint

**EN**
- The strongest first-wave endpoint shortlist is:
  - creatinine
  - USG
  - UPCR / proteinuria
  - systolic blood pressure
  - phosphorus
  - SDMA
- Among these, `proteinuria`, `phosphorus`, and `systolic blood pressure` are especially valuable because they bridge mechanism, prognosis, and treatment.
- The current core papers also support reading these endpoints on multiple lesion axes rather than as one undifferentiated renal-severity scale.

**ZH**
- 第一波核心终点指标短名单为：
  - 肌酐 (creatinine)
  - 尿比重 (USG)
  - UPCR / 蛋白尿 (proteinuria)
  - 收缩压 (systolic blood pressure)
  - 磷 (phosphorus)
  - SDMA
- 在这些指标中，**蛋白尿**、**磷**和**收缩压**尤其具有转化价值，因为它们连接了病理机制、预后评估和治疗。
- 当前的核心文献也支持在多个病变维度（轴向）上解读这些终点指标，而不是将其归结为单一的无差别肾功能严重程度评分。

### 转化层 / Translation

**EN**
- `Renal diet` is the clearest baseline-supported intervention in the current vault.
- Several interventions are clinically common but do not yet have equally strong supporting evidence in this corpus.
- The easiest overclaim is to confuse common use with strong efficacy evidence.
- The treatment layer is now also clearer at a disease-modification boundary: management and progression-control language are stronger than structural-reversal language.
- Early detection, risk recognition, and prevention-oriented thinking are now explicit parts of the vault, but still not strong enough to be treated as solved.
- The current core papers jointly support framing early detection as `serial surveillance`, not as a one-test biomarker problem.

**ZH**
- **肾脏处方粮**是当前知识库中获得最明确基础证据支持的干预措施。
- 几种临床上常见的干预手段在当前文献库中尚未获得同等强度的证据支持。
- 最容易出现的过度宣称，是将“临床常用”混淆为“有强效疗效证据支持”。
- 目前治疗层在“疾病修饰/逆转”的边界上更加清晰：疾病管理和延缓进展方面的陈述比“结构性逆转”的表述更有证据支撑。
- 早期检测、风险识别和预防导向的逻辑现在已成为本知识库的明确组成部分，但尚不足以被视为已解决的问题。
- 当前的核心文献共同支持将早期检测定义为**“连续追踪/监测”**，而非单一指标的生物标记物问题。

### 监管层 / Regulatory

**EN**
- There is no single generic “regulatory path.”
- China, FDA, EMA, and VMD each require different early strategic questions.
- FDA and EMA each contain a high-leverage special-route question that should be tested early rather than assumed.

**ZH**
- 不存在单一通用的“监管路径”。
- 中国、FDA、EMA 和 VMD 各自需要回答不同的早期战略问题。
- FDA 和 EMA 分别包含一个高杠杆的特殊通道问题，应当及早测试核实，而非主观假设。

## 2. 目前仍然薄弱的内容 / 2. What Still Feels Weak

### 薄弱层 1：模型架构 / Weak Layer 1: Model Architecture

**EN**
- The current “model layer” is still mostly natural-disease and observational logic.
- It is no longer purely observational, because the vault now has one explicit ischemic experimental anchor: [src-ckd-022](../../raw/papers/src-ckd-022.md)
- The real weakness now is not total absence, but lack of model density and taxonomy.

**ZH**
- 目前的“模型层”仍主要基于自然发生疾病和观察性逻辑。
- 这已不再是纯粹的观察研究，因为知识库现已包含了一个明确的缺血性实验模型锚点：[src-ckd-022](../../raw/papers/src-ckd-022.md)。
- 目前真正的薄弱点不在于完全空白，而在于缺乏模型的研究密度与系统分类。

### 薄弱层 2：SDMA 的定位 / Weak Layer 2: SDMA Positioning

**EN**
- SDMA is now more clearly supportable as a useful adjunctive biomarker linked to GFR-oriented early recognition logic.
- The remaining weakness is not whether SDMA matters at all, but how strongly to rank it against creatinine and USG across concrete use cases.
- This is now being collected into a dedicated working page: [sdma positioning](sdma-positioning.md)

**ZH**
- 目前，SDMA 作为与 GFR（肾小球滤过率）相关的早期识别逻辑相绑定的实用辅助生物标记物，获得了更明确的证据支持。
- 剩余的薄弱点不在于 SDMA 是否有用，而在于在具体的临床应用场景中，如何对其相较于肌酐和尿比重 (USG) 进行权重和排序。
- 这一议题目前正汇总于专用工作页面中：[SDMA 定位 / sdma-positioning](sdma-positioning.md)。

### 薄弱层 3：治疗手段排序 / Weak Layer 3: Treatment Ranking

**EN**
- We now have a treatment evidence matrix and a better minimum trial-outcome frame.
- What is still missing is a deeper intervention-by-intervention primary-study map.

**ZH**
- 我们现在拥有了治疗证据矩阵和更完善的临床试验最小结局指标框架。
- 目前仍然学术上缺失的是针对各种干预手段更深入的原始研究证据图谱。

### 薄弱层 4：特定适应症的监管细节 / Weak Layer 4: Indication-Specific Regulatory Detail

**EN**
- Current regulatory pages are route-level solid.
- They are not yet strong enough for a product-specific or submission-grade recommendation.

**ZH**
- 当前的监管页面在路径层面是可靠的。
- 但它们还不足以支撑针对特定产品或达到申报级别的策略推荐。

### 薄弱层 5：预防逻辑 / Weak Layer 5: Prevention Logic

**EN**
- The vault now has enough material to say prevention matters.
- It does not yet have enough material to say what a true prevention strategy should look like in practice.

**ZH**
- 知识库中目前的材料足以表明疾病预防的重要性。
- 但尚无足够材料来论述在实践中真正的预防策略应该如何落地实施。

### 薄弱层 6：从症状到临床诊断流程的转化 / Weak Layer 6: Feline Symptom-To-Workup Operationalization

**EN**
- The vault now has a clearer risk-and-recognition layer.
- It still does not define exactly when owner-observed change should trigger a standardized renal workup.

**ZH**
- 知识库目前拥有了更清晰的风险与识别层。
- 但仍未精确定义宠物主人观察到的哪些变化应当触发标准化的肾脏临床诊断工作流。

### 薄弱层 7：高血压治疗的细粒度细节 / Weak Layer 7: Hypertension Treatment Granularity

**EN**
- The vault now has a clearer hypertension/comorbidity layer.
- It is still stronger on why hypertension matters than on exact treatment-fork and monitoring-cadence detail.

**ZH**
- 知识库目前拥有了更清晰的高血压/合并症层。
- 但目前在阐述“高血压为何重要”上的论据要强于精确的药物治疗决策分支及长期监测频率的细节。

### 薄弱层 8：基于机器学习的早期检测宣称 / Weak Layer 8: Machine-Learning Early Detection Claims

**EN**
- The next likely pressure point is newer biomarker-plus-model literature.
- The vault now has one real metabolomics-plus-ML source: [src-ckd-018](../../raw/papers/src-ckd-018.md)
- That paper strengthens the idea of future panel-based augmentation, but it still does not justify replacing serial surveillance with a routine ML workflow.

**ZH**
- 下一个可能面临证据压力的方向是新兴的“生物标记物+算法模型”文献。
- 知识库目前包含了一篇真正的代谢组学联合机器学习的文献来源：[src-ckd-018](../../raw/papers/src-ckd-018.md)。
- 该论文强化了未来通过联合多指标检测包进行辅助诊断的设想，但仍不足以支持用日常的算法模型工作流来替代常规的连续临床追踪。

## 3. 最重要的跨层级桥梁 / 3. Most Important Cross-Layer Bridges

### 桥梁 1 / Bridge 1

`renal fibrosis -> phosphorus` (肾脏纤维化 → 磷)

- **EN:** Why it matters: this is not just a treatment issue, it is a progression and mechanism issue too.
- **ZH:** 为什么这一关联重要：这不仅是一个临床治疗问题，同样也是核心的病程进展与病理机制问题。

### 桥梁 2 / Bridge 2

`structural injury -> proteinuria` (结构性损伤 → 蛋白尿)

- **EN:** Why it matters: proteinuria belongs at the center of interpretation, not as a minor staging detail.
- **ZH:** 为什么这一关联重要：蛋白尿属于临床判读的核心，而非次要的分期细节。

### 桥梁 3 / Bridge 3

`vascular / glomerular injury context -> systolic blood pressure` (血管/肾小球损伤背景 → 收缩压)

- **EN:** Why it matters: blood pressure is a core disease signal and management lever.
- **ZH:** 为什么这一关联重要：血压是核心的疾病监测信号和长期管理杠杆。

### 桥梁 4 / Bridge 4

`route selection -> evidence package` (注册路径选择 → 证据包)

- **EN:** Why it matters: regulatory choice depends less on geography alone and more on product type + claim + feasible evidence package.
- **ZH:** 为什么这一关联重要：监管申报路径的选择并不完全取决于地域划分，而更多取决于产品类型、适应症宣称与可行的学术证据包。

## 4. 当前可以稳妥主张的内容 / 4. Current Arguments We Can Defend

以下论点目前已足够成熟，可用于内部学术讨论： / These are strong enough for internal discussion now:

- 在当前的知识库中，猫 CKD 最好被表述为以肾纤维化为中心的疾病。 / feline CKD is best framed as fibrosis-centered in the current vault
- 终点指标的选择不应被窄化为单一“肾脏标记物”的扁平思维。 / endpoint choice should not be flattened into a single “kidney marker” mindset
- 所有治疗总结表述必须标注循证级别/证据强度。 / treatment summaries must label evidence strength
- 高血压是核心共患病，而非边缘性问题。 / hypertension is central, not peripheral
- 在当前的文献库中，宠物主人观察到的多尿与多饮是具有实际临床识别意义的警示信号。 / owner-observed polyuria and polydipsia are meaningful recognition prompts in the current corpus
- 监管申报路径本质上是一个多分支的决策树问题。 / regulatory path is a branching decision problem
- 学术治疗试验的结局指标范围比临床日常的常规监测指标更广。 / treatment-trial outcomes are broader than routine clinical monitoring
- 自发性猫 CKD 在病理上仍应默认使用以老年猫为背景、以纤维化为中心的肾小管间质损伤框架。 / spontaneous feline CKD should still default to an aged-cat, fibrosis-centered tubulointerstitial framing
