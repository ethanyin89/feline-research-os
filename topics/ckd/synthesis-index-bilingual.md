### Mechanism / 机制

**EN**
- Renal fibrosis / tubulointerstitial fibrosis is the strongest current mechanism backbone.
- TGF-beta is the strongest current mediator-level expansion target, but still sits below fibrosis in certainty.

**ZH**
- 肾纤维化 / 肾小管间质纤维化是当前最稳的机制骨架。
- TGF-beta 是当前最值得扩展的 mediator-level target，但确定性仍低于 fibrosis 本身。

### Endpoints / 终点

**EN**
- The strongest first-wave endpoint shortlist is:
  - creatinine
  - USG
  - UPCR / proteinuria
  - systolic blood pressure
  - phosphorus
  - SDMA
- Proteinuria, phosphorus, and SBP are especially valuable because they bridge mechanism, prognosis, and treatment.

**ZH**
- 当前第一波最稳的终点短名单是：
  - creatinine
  - USG
  - UPCR / proteinuria
  - systolic blood pressure
  - phosphorus
  - SDMA
- 其中 proteinuria、phosphorus 和 SBP 特别重要，因为它们连接了机制、预后和治疗。

### Translation / 转化层

**EN**
- Renal diet is the clearest baseline-supported intervention.
- Early detection is better framed as serial surveillance than as a one-test biomarker solution.
- The easiest overclaim is to confuse common clinical use with strong evidence.

**ZH**
- renal diet 是当前支持度最清楚的基础干预。
- 早期识别更适合被表述为连续监测问题，而不是单一生物标志物解决方案。
- 最容易出现的过度表述，是把“临床常用”误写成“证据很强”。

### Regulatory / 监管层

**EN**
- There is no single generic regulatory path.
- China, FDA, EMA, and VMD require different early strategic questions.

**ZH**
- 不存在一个通用的监管路径。
- 中国、FDA、EMA 和 VMD 分别对应不同的前置战略问题。

## What We Can Defend / 当前可以稳妥主张的内容

**EN**
- feline CKD is best framed as fibrosis-centered
- endpoint interpretation should remain multi-axis
- hypertension is central, not peripheral
- owner-observed polyuria and polydipsia are meaningful recognition prompts

**ZH**
- feline CKD 最适合被表述为以纤维化为中心的疾病

- hypertension 是核心问题，不是外围问题
