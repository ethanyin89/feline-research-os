## 7. Current Best One-Sentence Summary

This vault is building a feline CKD research operating system where fibrosis is the mechanism backbone, a small endpoint set carries most of the practical value, symptom-level recognition and early detection are now explicit parts of the map, treatment evidence is uneven, and regulatory path depends on jurisdiction-specific branching rather than one generic answer.
