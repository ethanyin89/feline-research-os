# 什么是猫慢性肾病？ / What is Feline CKD?

## 简单回答 / Simple Answer

**猫慢性肾病（CKD）是指猫的肾脏长期受损，逐渐失去正常功能的疾病。**

肾脏的工作是过滤血液、排出废物、调节水分和矿物质。当肾脏受损后，这些功能会慢慢下降，废物在体内堆积，引起各种健康问题。

Feline chronic kidney disease (CKD) is a condition where a cat's kidneys become damaged over time and gradually lose their normal function.

The kidneys filter blood, remove waste, and regulate water and minerals. When damaged, these functions slowly decline, waste builds up in the body, and various health problems arise.

## 有多常见？ / How Common Is It?

CKD是老年猫最常见的疾病之一。

- **10岁以上**的猫中，约有**30-40%**患有某种程度的肾病
- 随着猫的寿命延长，CKD发病率也在增加
- 这是老年猫最常见的死亡原因之一

CKD is one of the most common diseases in older cats.

- About **30-40%** of cats over **10 years old** have some degree of kidney disease
- As cats live longer, CKD incidence increases
- It's one of the most common causes of death in older cats

## 有什么症状？ / What Are the Signs?

CKD的症状通常是渐进的，早期可能不明显：

CKD signs are usually gradual and may be subtle at first:

### 常见症状 / Common Signs

| 症状 | Sign |
|------|------|
| 喝水增多 | Increased thirst |
| 排尿增多 | Increased urination |
| 体重下降 | Weight loss |
| 食欲减退 | Decreased appetite |
| 呕吐 | Vomiting |
| 精神变差 | Lethargy |
| 毛发粗糙 | Poor coat condition |

### 晚期症状 / Late-Stage Signs

| 症状 | Sign |
|------|------|
| 口腔溃疡 | Mouth ulcers |
| 口臭 | Bad breath (ammonia smell) |
| 贫血（牙龈苍白） | Anemia (pale gums) |
| 严重脱水 | Severe dehydration |

**注意：** 这些症状也可能是其他疾病引起的。出现这些症状应咨询兽医。

**Note:** These signs can also be caused by other conditions. Consult a veterinarian if you notice these signs.

[source_supported_conclusion: src-ckd-004]

## 如何诊断？ / How Is It Diagnosed?

CKD诊断需要综合多项检查，不是只看一个数字：

CKD diagnosis requires multiple tests, not just one number:

| 检查项目 | 作用 |
|----------|------|
| 血液检查（肌酐、尿素氮） | 评估肾功能 |
| SDMA检测 | 可能更早发现肾功能下降 |
| 尿液检查（尿比重） | 评估肾脏浓缩能力 |
| 尿蛋白检查 | 评估肾脏损伤程度 |
| 血压测量 | 高血压与CKD相关 |
| 超声波 | 观察肾脏结构 |

| Test | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| Blood tests (creatinine, BUN) | Evaluate kidney function |
| SDMA test | May detect decline earlier |
| Urinalysis (specific gravity) | Evaluate concentration ability |
| Urine protein test | Assess kidney damage |
| Blood pressure | Hypertension linked to CKD |
| Ultrasound | View kidney structure |

[source_supported_conclusion: src-ckd-004, src-ckd-010]

## CKD分期 / CKD Staging

CKD分为4期（IRIS分期）：

CKD is classified into 4 stages (IRIS staging):

| 分期 | 肌酐水平 | 状态 |
|------|----------|------|
| 1期 | <1.6 mg/dL | 早期，通常无症状 |
| 2期 | 1.6-2.8 mg/dL | 轻度，可能有轻微症状 |
| 3期 | 2.9-5.0 mg/dL | 中度，明显症状 |
| 4期 | >5.0 mg/dL | 重度，严重症状 |

| Stage | Creatinine | Status |
|-------|------------|--------|
| Stage 1 | <1.6 mg/dL | Early, usually no symptoms |
| Stage 2 | 1.6-2.8 mg/dL | Mild, may have subtle signs |
| Stage 3 | 2.9-5.0 mg/dL | Moderate, obvious symptoms |
| Stage 4 | >5.0 mg/dL | Severe, serious symptoms |

**重要：** 分期还要结合蛋白尿程度和血压来综合评估。

**Important:** Staging also considers proteinuria and blood pressure.

## 可以治疗吗？ / Can It Be Treated?

**CKD目前无法治愈，但可以管理。** 目标是减缓病情进展、控制症状、提高生活质量。

**CKD cannot be cured, but it can be managed.** Goals are to slow progression, control symptoms, and improve quality of life.

### 管理措施 / Management Options

| 措施 | 作用 |
|------|------|
| 肾脏处方粮 | 最重要的基础管理，限制磷和蛋白质 |
| 磷结合剂 | 当饮食控制不够时使用 |
| 抗高血压药 | 控制血压，保护肾脏 |
| 补液治疗 | 维持水分平衡 |
| 抗恶心药 | 控制呕吐和食欲问题 |

| Measure | Purpose |
|---------|---------|
| Renal diet | Most important base management; limits phosphorus and protein |
| Phosphate binders | When diet alone isn't enough |
| Blood pressure medication | Control hypertension, protect kidneys |
| Fluid therapy | Maintain hydration |
| Anti-nausea medication | Control vomiting and appetite issues |

**关键：** 肾脏处方粮是最有证据支持的管理措施。

**Key point:** Renal diet has the strongest evidence support for management.

[source_supported_conclusion: src-ckd-003, src-ckd-004]

## 预防和早期发现 / Prevention and Early Detection

| 建议 | Recommendation |
|------|----------------|
| 7岁以上定期体检 | Regular checkups after age 7 |
| 包括血液和尿液检查 | Including blood and urine tests |
| 监测体重变化 | Monitor weight changes |
| 观察饮水和排尿习惯 | Watch drinking and urination habits |

早期发现可以更早开始管理，可能延长生存时间。

Early detection allows earlier management and may extend survival time.

[source_supported_conclusion: src-ckd-004]

## 下一步 / Next Step

**如果你担心猫可能有肾病：**

1. 安排兽医检查，包括血液和尿液检测
2. 带上猫的饮食、饮水、排尿习惯记录
3. 不要自行限制蛋白质或改变饮食

**If you're concerned your cat may have kidney disease:**

1. Schedule a veterinary exam including blood and urine tests
2. Bring notes on diet, drinking, and urination habits
3. Do not restrict protein or change diet on your own

**请注意：** 早期CKD可能没有明显症状。定期体检是最好的早期发现方式。

**Note:** Early CKD may have no obvious symptoms. Regular checkups are the best way to catch it early.
