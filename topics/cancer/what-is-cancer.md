# 什么是猫癌症？ / What is Feline Cancer?

## 简单回答 / Simple Answer

**猫癌症是指猫体内细胞异常生长、失去正常控制的疾病。癌细胞可以侵犯周围组织，有时会扩散到身体其他部位。**

Feline cancer is a disease where cells in a cat's body grow abnormally and out of control. Cancer cells can invade surrounding tissues and sometimes spread to other parts of the body.

[source_supported_conclusion: src-cancer-004]

## 猫癌症常见吗？ / How Common Is It?

癌症是老年猫的常见疾病。猫的平均寿命延长意味着癌症发病率也在增加。

Cancer is common in older cats. As cats live longer on average, cancer incidence has increased.

**常见类型 / Common Types:**

| 类型 | 简单说明 |
|------|----------|
| 淋巴瘤 | 最常见的血液系统肿瘤，可发生在肠道、胸腔或全身多处 |
| 乳腺癌 | 未绝育母猫高发，通常恶性 |
| 口腔鳞状细胞癌 | 口腔最常见肿瘤，难以治疗 |
| 注射部位肉瘤 | 与注射有关的罕见肿瘤 |
| 皮肤肿瘤 | 包括鳞状细胞癌、肥大细胞瘤等 |

| Type | Simple Description |
|------|---------------------|
| Lymphoma | Most common blood cancer, can occur in intestines, chest, or throughout body |
| Mammary carcinoma | Common in unspayed females, usually malignant |
| Oral squamous cell carcinoma | Most common oral tumor, difficult to treat |
| Injection-site sarcoma | Rare tumor associated with injections |
| Skin tumors | Including squamous cell carcinoma, mast cell tumors |

[source_supported_conclusion: src-cancer-002, src-cancer-004]

## 有什么症状？ / What Are the Signs?

癌症症状因类型和位置不同而各异。常见警示信号包括：

Cancer signs vary depending on type and location. Common warning signs include:

| 症状 | Sign |
|------|------|
| 体重下降 | Weight loss |
| 食欲减退 | Loss of appetite |
| 持续呕吐或腹泻 | Persistent vomiting or diarrhea |
| 不愈合的伤口 | Non-healing wounds |
| 异常肿块 | Unusual lumps or swellings |
| 呼吸困难 | Difficulty breathing |
| 口腔异味或流涎 | Bad breath or drooling |

**注意：** 这些症状也可能是其他疾病引起的。出现这些症状应咨询兽医。

**Note:** These signs can also be caused by other conditions. Consult a veterinarian if you notice these signs.

[source_supported_conclusion: src-cancer-040]

## 如何诊断？ / How Is It Diagnosed?

癌症诊断通常需要多个步骤：

Cancer diagnosis usually involves several steps:

1. **体检** — 兽医检查肿块、淋巴结、整体状况
2. **影像** — X光、超声波、CT扫描查看肿瘤位置和大小
3. **组织检查** — 细针穿刺或活检确定肿瘤类型
4. **分期** — 确定癌症是否扩散

1. **Physical exam** — Veterinarian checks for lumps, lymph nodes, overall condition
2. **Imaging** — X-rays, ultrasound, CT to see tumor location and size
3. **Tissue sampling** — Fine needle aspirate or biopsy to determine tumor type
4. **Staging** — Determine if cancer has spread

[source_supported_conclusion: src-cancer-040]

## 可以治疗吗？ / Can It Be Treated?

许多猫癌症可以治疗，但结果因类型、分期和个体情况而异。

Many feline cancers can be treated, but outcomes vary by type, stage, and individual factors.

**治疗方式 / Treatment Options:**

| 方式 | 适用情况 |
|------|----------|
| 手术 | 局部肿瘤的首选治疗 |
| 化疗 | 淋巴瘤等系统性癌症 |
| 放疗 | 某些无法手术的肿瘤 |
| 姑息治疗 | 控制症状、提高生活质量 |

| Option | When Used |
|--------|-----------|
| Surgery | First choice for localized tumors |
| Chemotherapy | Systemic cancers like lymphoma |
| Radiation | Some non-surgical tumors |
| Palliative care | Symptom control, quality of life |

**重要提示：** 猫的化疗与人不同，副作用通常较轻。

**Important:** Chemotherapy in cats differs from humans; side effects are usually milder.

[source_supported_conclusion: src-cancer-040]

## 预防 / Prevention

虽然不能完全预防癌症，但以下措施可能有帮助：

While cancer cannot be completely prevented, these measures may help:

| 措施 | Measure |
|------|---------|
| 早期绝育 | 显著降低乳腺癌风险 |
| 避免阳光暴晒 | 白猫皮肤癌风险较高 |
| 定期体检 | 早发现早治疗 |
| 减少注射部位刺激 | 注射部位肉瘤预防 |

| Measure | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| Early spaying | Significantly reduces mammary cancer risk |
| Avoid sun exposure | White cats have higher skin cancer risk |
| Regular checkups | Early detection, early treatment |
| Reduce injection site irritation | Injection-site sarcoma prevention |

2. Schedule a veterinary examination
3. Do not self-diagnose or delay seeking care
