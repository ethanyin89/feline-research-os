# FDA CVM

## Why It Matters

FDA CVM anchors the US branch of the regulatory layer, where route selection depends on the product archetype, claim, evidence package, and whether any special pathway is actually available.

## Current Notes

- FDA should not be simplified into a single default path in the current vault.
- Companion-animal study design and route eligibility questions need to be asked early.
- This card is currently route-level, not indication-specific.
