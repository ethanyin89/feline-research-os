# Mutation-Origin Logic

## Why It Matters

Mutation-origin logic matters because it is one of the central mechanism anchors in the current FIP vault, but it is also one of the easiest branches to misuse diagnostically.

## Current Notes

- The current vault supports mutation-origin as a mechanism-emergence claim first.
- More specific refinements such as the `3c` branch now suggest that gut competence and systemic disease competence are not the same thing.
- It should not be promoted directly into mutation-test certainty.
- Its strongest current job is to explain why FIP should be modeled as disease emergence from background coronavirus ecology.
