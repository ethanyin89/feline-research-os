# AIM

## Why It Matters

AIM remains on the map because it is biologically interesting and may matter for feline renal research, but the current CKD vault does not yet support promoting it into the top mechanism layer.

## Current Notes

- AIM is not currently a primary CKD mechanism anchor in this vault.
- Fibrosis and TGF-beta are much better supported by the current feline CKD corpus.
- AIM should be treated as a watchlist mechanism until direct feline CKD evidence is ingested and compiled.
