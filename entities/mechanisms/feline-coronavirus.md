# Feline Coronavirus Background

## Why It Matters

Feline coronavirus background matters because the current FIP vault treats disease emergence as something that grows out of endemic coronavirus ecology, not as a flat exposure-equals-disease story.

## Current Notes

- In the current FIP module, feline coronavirus background and FIP itself should not be collapsed into one concept.
- The strongest current role of this entity is to anchor the distinction between endemic background and systemic disease emergence.
- This entity belongs in the mechanism layer first, not in the diagnostic-certainty layer.
