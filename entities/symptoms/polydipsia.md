# Polydipsia

## Why It Matters

Polydipsia matters in this vault because increased drinking is one of the clearest owner-observed changes that may precede formal CKD diagnosis in cats.

## Current Notes

- Polydipsia is a symptom-level recognition clue, not a disease-specific proof of CKD.
- In the current corpus, it matters most when paired with age, risk context, and follow-up workup rather than read in isolation.
- Like polyuria, it belongs in the CKD recognition workflow because delayed action on this kind of change appears to be part of the practical disease problem.
