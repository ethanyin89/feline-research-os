# Polyuria

## Why It Matters

Polyuria matters in this vault because owner-observed urination change may appear before formal CKD diagnosis and should act as an early-recognition trigger in older or high-risk cats.

## Current Notes

- Polyuria is a recognition signal, not a CKD-specific mechanism marker.
- In the current corpus, its strongest value is helping explain why diagnosis may be happening later than it should.
- Polyuria should trigger renal workup consideration when it appears in a mature, geriatric, or otherwise high-risk cat.
