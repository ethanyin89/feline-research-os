# Chronic Enteropathy

## Why It Matters

Chronic enteropathy matters because it is the wider operational frame that currently holds feline IBD, food-responsive disease, and part of the lymphoma-boundary workup together.

## Current Notes

- In the current vault, chronic enteropathy is the most practical recognition and activity-tracking frame.
- FCEAI belongs to chronic-enteropathy management more than to strict disease-class separation.
- Fibrosis, burden markers, and noninvasive stool markers all currently make more sense inside the chronic-enteropathy layer than inside a narrow idiopathic-IBD-only frame.
