# Muscularis Thickening

## Why It Matters

Muscularis thickening matters because it is the clearest current imaging signal that pushes the feline IBD workup toward stronger lymphoma concern.

## Current Notes

- In the current vault, muscularis thickening is a lymphoma-leaning imaging signal.
- It should be used as suspicion-shaping support, not as stand-alone diagnosis.
- It sits above generic ultrasound abnormality but below biopsy and histopathology authority.
