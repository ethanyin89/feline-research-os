# Anaemia

## Why It Matters

Anaemia is a useful context endpoint in feline CKD because it tracks systemic burden and is linked to more advanced structural disease in the current corpus.

## Current Notes

- Anaemia is not a first-wave diagnostic anchor for CKD.
- It becomes important when disease burden and structural injury are already substantial.
- In the current vault, anaemia is best treated as a pathology-linked context marker and a treatment-context variable.
