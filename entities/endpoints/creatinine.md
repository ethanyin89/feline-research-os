# Creatinine

## Why It Matters

Creatinine remains a first-wave CKD endpoint because it anchors routine diagnosis, IRIS staging, and longitudinal monitoring in the current feline corpus.

## Current Notes

- Creatinine is part of the operational CKD core, especially when combined with USG and persistence over time.
- A persistent increase greater than 15% from prior baseline can matter even before a very advanced picture is obvious.
- Creatinine is practical and entrenched, but it is not an ideal earliest-detection marker.
- In the current vault, creatinine is strongest as a routine working marker, not as the single best representation of structural disease.
