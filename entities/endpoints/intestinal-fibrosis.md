# Intestinal Fibrosis

## Why It Matters

Intestinal fibrosis matters because it is now one of the clearest structural chronicity signals in the feline IBD vault.

## Current Notes

- Intestinal fibrosis belongs in a chronicity-and-burden branch rather than in a generic pathology afterthought.
- It should be kept separate from both inflammatory activity and support-marker abnormality.
- Current evidence supports its relevance to disease burden, but not yet a ready-made intervention pathway.
