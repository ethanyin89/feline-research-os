# Metabolomics

## Why It Matters

Metabolomics matters because it is currently the strongest frontier-separation signal in the feline IBD vault.

## Current Notes

- In the current vault, metabolomics belongs to a frontier-marker layer above shared support markers.
- It currently looks more promising for future stratification and biologic separation than for routine-ready diagnosis.
- It should remain below exclusion-first workup, imaging, biopsy-site strategy, and pathology.
