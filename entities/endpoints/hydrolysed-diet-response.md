# Hydrolysed Diet Response

## Why It Matters

Hydrolysed diet response matters because it is the cleanest current early practical treatment anchor in the feline IBD vault.

## Current Notes

- In the current vault, hydrolysed diet response anchors the diet-first branch of chronic-enteropathy management.
- It should not be overinterpreted as idiopathic-IBD-specific proof because the evidence still overlaps with food-responsive disease.
- It belongs in the treatment layer, but remains subordinate to exclusion-first diagnostic architecture.
