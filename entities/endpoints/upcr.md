# UPCR

## Why It Matters

UPCR is a core CKD bridge endpoint because it connects prognosis, substaging, progression logic, and the hypertension branch of disease interpretation.

## Current Notes

- Proteinuria should not be treated as a minor lab detail in feline CKD.
- The current corpus repeatedly links proteinuria to progression and to hypertension-related injury logic.
- UPCR belongs in both the endpoint layer and the hypertension/comorbidity layer.
- The newer proteinuric-kidney-disease study strengthens the idea that UPCR can also point toward different disease compartments, especially glomerular disease, rather than acting as a generic renal severity number.
- High UPC values are important, but they do not identify exact pathology by themselves.
