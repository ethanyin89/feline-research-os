# Fecal S100A12

## Why It Matters

Fecal S100A12 matters because it is the clearest current noninvasive inflammatory support marker in the feline IBD vault.

## Current Notes

- In the current vault, fecal S100A12 behaves as a disease-versus-health support marker rather than an IBD-versus-lymphoma separator.
- It is useful because it offers noninvasive inflammatory signal without trying to replace tissue-centered workup.
- It should remain below biopsy, imaging, and pathology authority.
