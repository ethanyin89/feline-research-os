# FCEAI

## Why It Matters

FCEAI matters because it is the strongest current operational activity-and-response endpoint in the feline IBD vault.

## Current Notes

- FCEAI belongs to chronic-enteropathy management, not to disease-class discrimination.
- It currently works best as an activity and response-tracking layer above routine monitoring.
- It should not be promoted into an IBD-versus-lymphoma separator.
