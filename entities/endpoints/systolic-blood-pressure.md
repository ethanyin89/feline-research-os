# Systolic Blood Pressure

## Why It Matters

SBP is a core feline CKD endpoint because it affects substaging, target-organ-damage risk, proteinuria interpretation, and treatment decisions.

## Current Notes

- CKD and hypertension in cats have an intermingled relationship rather than a simple one-way association.
- SBP should be treated as a first-wave endpoint, not a background variable.
- A practical target below 160 mmHg appears in the current hypertension review and guideline logic.
- Blood pressure monitoring is underused in CKD cats, so missing SBP data often means missing critical context.
