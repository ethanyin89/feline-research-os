# SDMA

## Why It Matters

SDMA matters because it sits in the current vault's early-detection conversation, where it may help extend recognition beyond a creatinine-only mindset.

## Current Notes

- SDMA is provisionally part of the first-wave endpoint shortlist in this vault.
- Its strongest current role is early-detection and screening support, not replacement of the classic diagnostic core.
- The current corpus still does not rank SDMA against creatinine and USG with enough confidence to be definitive.
- SDMA is therefore active in the vault, but still marked by real extraction uncertainty.

## Current Best Positioning

- It should not currently be written as a standalone screening shortcut.
- The best current frontier story is panel augmentation, not SDMA-alone replacement.
